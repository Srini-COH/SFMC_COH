Facility practices
  • We continue to use enhanced cleaning and disinfecting practices at each cancer center, while also following steps to help enable social distancing.
  • Universal masking is still strictly enforced.
COVID-19 screenings
  • Our cancer centers have a single point of entry, with mandatory screenings of all patients, physicians, employees, caregivers and visitors.
Visitation guidelines
  • Each patient is allowed one caregiver in outpatient areas of the cancer center, and no one under the age of 18 is permitted at any site.
  • At some locations, caregivers are not allowed into inpatient units, pre-op and PACU areas.
