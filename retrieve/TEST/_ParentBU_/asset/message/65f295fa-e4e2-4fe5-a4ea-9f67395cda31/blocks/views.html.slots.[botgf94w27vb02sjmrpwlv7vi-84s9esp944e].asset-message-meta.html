Dear Respected, Valued and Loved Patients, Cancer Fighters and Caregivers,

Several years ago, we embarked on a path with the bold vision to expand the reach of the Mother Standard® of care to hundreds of thousands of patients, caregivers and others in need. During this journey, we identified a nationally renowned organization that shares our values and determination to give people the chance to live longer, better and more fully through their battle with cancer.  
 
Today, we are pleased to share our intention to join City of Hope®, a California-based National Cancer Institute-designated organization with a century-long reputation of compassionate patient care and cutting-edge research. While we await several key government and regulatory approvals for this to take place, I wanted to be the first to let you know of our intentions and the profoundly positive impact this will have on you, both organizations, and the transformation of the future of cancer care. 
 
We are confident that through this joining of forces, patients will experience greater access to clinical trials and research and the physicians who lead them, expanded services and treatment options, and additional resources for new innovations in cancer care delivery and technology. Your physicians and care teams will not change and will help evolve the combined shared culture and mission to provide quality and compassionate care for people fighting cancer.  
 
We are excited about the opportunity to combine our over three decades of unparalleled patient experience and quality care with City of Hope’s century-long dedication to combining science with humanity. We will keep you updated as we make additional progress toward this exciting next chapter in the history of CTCA.  
 
As always, we are grateful that you have entrusted us with your care and for allowing us the honor to fight alongside you in your cancer journey. 


Gratefully,

Pat A. Basu, M.D.
President & CEO
Cancer Treatment Centers of America®
