Cancer Fighters® invites you to participate in their Holiday Card Exchange! Help spread seasonal cheer by writing a festive note of hope, joy and inspiration to an individual who is experiencing a cancer journey.

Here's how it works: Sign up to join in the fun by November 21. You'll then receive a blank holiday card in the mail to fill out. Return it to the Cancer Fighters Team in the provided postage-paid envelope, then you'll receive a holiday card this December from someone else through the exchange.
