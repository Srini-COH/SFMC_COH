Sign up to use the MyCityofHope patient portal

Using MyCityofHope, you can:

  • Communicate with your care team.
  • Request appointments.
  • Check most test, scan and lab results.
  • See your complete medication list and easily request refills.
  • Get personalized health care reminders.

Before your upcoming appointment:

  1. Sign up for the MyCityofHope patient portal.

  2. Complete the PreCheck-In within the portal, prior to your appointment.
