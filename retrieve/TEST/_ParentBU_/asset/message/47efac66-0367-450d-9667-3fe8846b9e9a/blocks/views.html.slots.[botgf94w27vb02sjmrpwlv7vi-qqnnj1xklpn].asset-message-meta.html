Here are some easy ways you can recognize the caregivers among us, whether they're helping you or someone else:

  • Thank them. Make your favorite caregivers a meal, send them a card or email them with words of gratitude.
  • Offer help. Taking a break is essential to keeping up the energy required of being a caregiver. Help them figure out how they can get away for a few hours.
  • Spend time. A phone call or visit can make a big difference to someone who might be feeling isolated and overworked.

Throughout the challenges you may face together, it's important to have the support of your loved ones and remember they will need support, too. Check out these 10 tips for keeping relationships strong through the cancer journey for ideas on how to support one another.
