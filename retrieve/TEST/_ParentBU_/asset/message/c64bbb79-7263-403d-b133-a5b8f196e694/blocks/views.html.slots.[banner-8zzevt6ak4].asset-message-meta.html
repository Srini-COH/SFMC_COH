Fatigue is one of the most common side effects of cancer treatment, affecting more than 70% of patients. If left untreated, fatigue may cause you to miss appointments or delay your cancer treatment overall.

Since the causes of fatigue vary, it's important that your care team is trained in therapies to treat your specific needs. At City of Hope®, our team of experts is trained to identify what's causing your fatigue and address it with supportive therapies.
