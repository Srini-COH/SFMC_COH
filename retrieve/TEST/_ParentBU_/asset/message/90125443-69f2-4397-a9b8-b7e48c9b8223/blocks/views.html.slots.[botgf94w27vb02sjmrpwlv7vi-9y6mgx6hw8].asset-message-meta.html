No matter where you are in your cancer journey, cancer screenings should be on your calendar. As you may know, screening is one of the most powerful tools available to reduce cancer risk, including the risk of a recurrence or second cancer.

If you’re like more than two-thirds of Americans*, you delayed or cancelled screening appointments during the COVID-19 pandemic. While your doctor may recommend waiting on certain screenings if you’re in active treatment, you can book appointments in advance. It’s much easier to reschedule a screening than to remember to book it.
