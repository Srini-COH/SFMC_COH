We are very pleased to announce that Cancer Treatment Centers of America is now officially part of City of Hope, a National Cancer Institute-designated comprehensive care center.

This joining of forces unites our shared vision, values and commitment to broaden access to high-quality, compassionate care that puts patients first. We are excited about the opportunity to expand your access to world-class cancer care, clinical trials, groundbreaking research and innovations in cancer treatments.

We want to assure you that your existing care remains unchanged. Your physicians and care teams, care location, scheduled appointments and support services will all be here to continue supporting you through your journey, and you will experience the same Mother Standard® of care that you deserve.
