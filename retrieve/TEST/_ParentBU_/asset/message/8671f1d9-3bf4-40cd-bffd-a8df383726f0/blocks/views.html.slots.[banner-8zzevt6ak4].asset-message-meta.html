Pain, whether caused by cancer or a side effect of treatment, can cause significant challenges. Aside from the day-to-day disruptions, that discomfort, if left untreated, may interfere with treatment schedules.

Many patients don't know that pain management is a distinct branch of medicine focused on reducing pain and helping to improve quality of life. At City of Hope®, controlling pain is an essential and highly valued component of the care we offer all our patients.
