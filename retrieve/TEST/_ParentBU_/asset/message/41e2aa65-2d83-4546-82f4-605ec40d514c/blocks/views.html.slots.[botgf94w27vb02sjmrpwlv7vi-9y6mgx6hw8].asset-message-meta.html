November is Caregiver Appreciation Month! It's an occasion worth celebrating many times throughout the year, and this month is a great reminder to reflect and say thank you to those who help us through so much.

The caregiver is an integral member of a cancer patient's care team. At Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA), we recognize the importance of having your loved ones involved, which is why we offer caregiver support services. We engage your caregivers in discussions about your diagnosis and treatment plan and help to coordinate appointments, offer counseling and more.
