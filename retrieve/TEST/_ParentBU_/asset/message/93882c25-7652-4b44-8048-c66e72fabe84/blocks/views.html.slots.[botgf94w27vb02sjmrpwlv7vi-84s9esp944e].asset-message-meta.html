CTCA® teams are working around the clock to bring necessary care to those who need it. In addition to taking every precautionary measure to ensure their health and safety, we are rapidly enhancing telehealth capabilities across our hospital network to protect patients and hospital teams, while still keeping patients connected with their providers and maintaining continuity of their care.

Our providers are diligently working with their patients to minimize disruption for those who need to be onsite for their critical care and, at the same time, reviewing every appointment to identify where alternative options should be suggested.
