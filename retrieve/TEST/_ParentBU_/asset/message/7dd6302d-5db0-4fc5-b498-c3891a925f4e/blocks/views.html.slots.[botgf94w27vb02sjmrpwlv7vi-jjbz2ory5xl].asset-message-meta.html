Facility practices:
  • We have expanded our already-rigorous cleaning and disinfecting practices.
  • We have removed and spaced furniture throughout each cancer center to enable social distancing.
  • Universal masking is strictly enforced. We are providing masks for all patients and caregivers to wear throughout their time in any of our facilities and until they get back home.
  • All CTCA employees are provided the appropriate mask based on their patient responsibilities.
Screening:
  • Our cancer centers have implemented a single point of entry, with 24-hour, mandatory screening of all patients and caregivers.
  • All CTCA employees are self-screening for symptoms twice a day to protect the health and safety of the patients in their care.
Visitation guidelines:
  • Each patient is allowed one caregiver over the age of 18 in outpatient areas of the cancer center.
  • At some locations, caregivers are not allowed into inpatient units, pre-op and PACU areas. In those facilities, these areas are accessible only to necessary employees and providers.
