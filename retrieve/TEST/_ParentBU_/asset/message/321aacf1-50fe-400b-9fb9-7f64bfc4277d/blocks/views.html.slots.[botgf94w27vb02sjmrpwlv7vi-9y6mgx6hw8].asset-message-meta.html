As someone who has experienced the life-changing news of a cancer diagnosis, you know the importance of maintaining consistent cancer screenings.

While your oncologist is working with you on prevention efforts against risk factors for recurrence or a second cancer, you can help inform and empower others with prevention measures that they can take as well.

In recognition of Cancer Prevention Month this February, help share the importance of cancer screenings with friends, family and loved ones while also making them a priority for yourself.

Start by keeping track of your recommended screenings and family history in one convenient document using the Cancer Screening Tracker from Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA).
