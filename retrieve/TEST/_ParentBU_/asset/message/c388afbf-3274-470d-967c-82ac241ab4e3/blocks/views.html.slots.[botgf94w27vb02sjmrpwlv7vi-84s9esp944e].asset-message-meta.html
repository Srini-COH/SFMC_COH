Dear City of Hope patient,

The federal COVID-19 Public Health Emergency declaration ended on May 11, 2023. This date impacts many areas of our care, including a change to the resources we have provided to our patients and caregivers.

Many City of Hope Phoenix patients and caregivers received pandemic-related travel and accommodations benefits during the public health emergency. In alignment with the end of the COVID-19 Public Health Emergency, we will return to prepandemic benefits rates beginning August 1, 2023. Note, this will not impact rates for appointments prior to August 1, 2023, or for reservations already booked as of July 12, 2023.

We understand that each situation is unique and encourage you to contact Patient Travel at 623-207-3800 or Guest Accommodations at 623-207-3530 with any questions.

Thank you for entrusting your care with City of Hope Phoenix.

Kevin Tulipana, D.O., M.S.
President, City of Hope Phoenix
