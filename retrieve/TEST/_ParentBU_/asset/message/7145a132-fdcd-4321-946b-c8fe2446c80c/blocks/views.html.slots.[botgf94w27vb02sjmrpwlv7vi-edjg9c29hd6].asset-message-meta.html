The goal of immunotherapy in oncology is to control the disease by using your immune system to find and attack cancer cells. Learn more about immunotherapy for cancer: How it works, who's a candidate and where to get it.

Immunotherapy treatment is an option only for some patients with certain types of cancer. The treatment may also work better for some patients than others, so if you undergo immunotherapy, your oncologist would closely monitor your progress and may recommend the treatment be used in combination with other options.

At Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA), your care team will develop a recommended treatment plan and work with you to decide whether immunotherapy or other treatment options are right for you, helping you make informed decisions about your care.
