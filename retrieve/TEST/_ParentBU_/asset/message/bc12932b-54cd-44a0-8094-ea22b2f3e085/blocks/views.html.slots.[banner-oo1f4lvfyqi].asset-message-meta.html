Many patients worry about how cancer will impact their lives, from how they physically feel to how it will affect their loved ones.

At Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA), your team of experts treats the disease while also giving you the tools to maintain your strength, stamina and quality of life.

This integrative approach is designed to help you get the most out of life before, during and after treatment. The goal of integrative cancer care is to help you better tolerate treatment-related side effects to reduce or avoid delays or interruptions in your treatment, so you can focus on healing.

Who would be part of my care team?
Some members of your integrative care team, like the medical or radiation oncologist, are dedicated to treating the cancer. Others, like the pain management physician or oncology rehabilitation therapist, will help you learn about and manage your side effects through supportive care therapies, to help you feel better through your journey to get better.

How can I get started?
Your next step in getting a personalized treatment plan from CTCA® is to schedule your in-person evaluation. This appointment will allow our experts to assess your needs and develop a care plan with you while you get a feel for the hospital and see what it would be like to become a patient.
