It's important to talk to your doctor about any pain you're experiencing so he or she can try to help you manage it. Learn the 5 facts you should know about managing cancer-related pain.

Having clinicians on your care team who focus on evaluating, treating and managing acute (short-term) or chronic (long-term) pain could help you find relief. Patients at Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) have pain management services available as part of a personalized treatment plan for integrative care that focuses on healing the whole person.
