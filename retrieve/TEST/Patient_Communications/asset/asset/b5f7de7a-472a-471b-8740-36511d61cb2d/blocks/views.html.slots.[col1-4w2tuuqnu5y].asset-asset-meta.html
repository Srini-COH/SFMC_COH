Visiting for your evaluation

You will meet your care team, learn about our services and receive your individualized treatment plan.

Your Evaluation

On your first day, you will meet with members of your care team to determine the diagnostic testing needed to confirm the results noted in your medical records. These tests, and any additional appointments with specialists, will be conducted throughout your visit. This information will help your care team develop a treatment plan personalized to your individual cancer type and needs.

Once all of the testing has been completed and your personalized treatment plan created, you will meet with your care team to discuss treatment options. During this discussion, you will have the opportunity to ask any questions you may have about your options so that you can choose the treatment plan that works best for you. At this time, you can also decide when you want to begin treatment.

Amenities

Onsite Services
CTCA Tulsa offers a variety of services and amenities onsite to help make your visit as convenient as possible. These include:.

  • Lily Bella Spa and Salon
  • Activity rooms with puzzles, games, computers, big-screen TV, pool table and sewing machines
  • Pharmacy and Gift Shop
  • Computers and work stations throughout the hospital
  • Library
  • Fitness Center
  • Chapel

Dining
At CTCA, licensed and registered dietitians work closely with the culinary team to develop high quality, health-conscious menus that feature delicious and nutritious meals at affordable prices, including:

  • A wide variety of seasonal produce
  • Whole grains
  • Lean proteins, such as chicken, turkey, beef and wild-caught fish
  • Diabetic friendly
  • Gluten-free
  • Low-fat

We know that cancer treatment may affect your appetite and food preferences, so let us know if you require something special. We will make every effort to accommodate your request.

Activities
To help you pass the time between appointments and consultations, we offer patients and caregivers a variety of activities, such as bingo, craft classes and support groups.

Cancer Fighters® Program
The Cancer Fighters® program is a great way to stay connected to fellow CTCA patients and caregivers while at the hospital and at home. As a member, you receive helpful communications, including education from our clinical experts and updates on upcoming events at our hospital and in your hometown. Be sure to visit Cancer Fighters Corner in the dining room to learn more.

New Section

To prepare for your evaluation, your medical oncologist reviews your medical records and notes from your Nurse Navigator. Your clinical situation is also presented to an integrative team that represents different areas of expertise. This will ensure that your needs are met with the various modalities and that your visit is productive.
