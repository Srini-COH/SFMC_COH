Traveling to CTCA

We strive to make traveling to our hospital easy and affordable for you and a travel companion.

Transportation

By Air
We are pleased to assist you with your transportation arrangements to and from Tulsa International Airport. 

By Car
If you plan to drive to CTCA Tulsa, we can provide you with directions from your location or you can map directions yourself.

Around Town – Shuttle Service
The CTCA shuttle route and schedule will be posted in the hospital.

Lodging and Accommodations

Our goal is to provide convenient, comfortable and affordable accommodations for all CTCA patients.

CTCA Guest Room
Initial Evaluation (per night) $25*
During Treatment (per night) $50

RV Accommodations
A special area of our parking lot is designated for RV's and includes customer amenities, such as electricity, water and plumbing hook ups, for $25 per night.

Our onsite rooms include standard hotel amenities, plus:

  • Oversized, ADA-compliant bathrooms (walk-in shower available upon request)
  • Compact refrigerator with freezer
  • Free wireless Internet access
  • Access to a shared laundry room with complimentary washing machines, dryers, irons and ironing boards

Please note: CTCA Tulsa is a tobacco-free campus (including cigarettes, cigars, pipes, smokeless tobacco, e-cigarettes or other tobacco products).

* If you are a beneficiary of a government health insurance program, such as Medicare, CTCA is not permitted to provide travel, transportation or lodging benefits, including discounted hotel rates. However, we will do everything we can to assist you in coordinating your travel and finding affordable lodging.
