Welcome

On behalf of everyone at Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) at Eastern Regional Medical Center (Eastern), welcome to the CTCA® family.

We are honored that you have entrusted us with your care. Our promise is to provide you with what we call the Mother Standard ® of care – the kind of care that each of us wants for our own families and loved ones.

Our team includes more than 700 highly skilled clinicians, medical professionals and support staff, all of whom are dedicated to supporting you on your path of healing and hope. Our cancer experts will work with you to tailor a treatment plan to meet your individual needs. Our unique approach to cancer treatment combines advancements in genomic testing and precision cancer treatment, surgery, radiation, immunotherapy and chemotherapy, with evidence-informed supportive therapies designed to help patients by enhancing their quality of life while managing side effects.

Throughout your experience with us, we invite and encourage your feedback. Whether it’s in a survey, during meetings with your medical team or as part of our Cancer Fighters® program, we want to know what you think and how we can improve your experience. We understand that cancer treatment is not a journey anyone wants to travel, but our goal is to do everything possible to provide you with the finest care and best possible experience.

We are here for you. If I can ever be of help, please let me know.

Nancy G. Hesse, MSN, RN
President and Chief Executive Officer
CTCA at Eastern Regional Medical Center
