Visiting for your evaluation

You will meet your care team, learn about our services and receive your individualized treatment plan.

Your Evaluation

On your first day, you will meet with your care team to determine the diagnostic testing needed to confirm the results noted in your medical records. These tests, and any additional appointments with specialists, will be conducted throughout your visit. This information will help your care team develop a treatment plan personalized to your individual cancer type and needs.

Once all of the testing has been completed and your personalized treatment plan created, you will meet with your care team to discuss treatment options. During this discussion, you will have the opportunity to ask any questions you may have about your options so that you can choose the treatment plan that works best for you. At this time, you can also decide when you want to begin treatment.

Amenities

Guest Services
Our guest services team is available to you and your caregiver should you need assistance with day-to-day tasks, such as mailing letters, faxing and copying services and reserving car rentals. This team can also help you plan outings within the Goodyear/Phoenix area.

Dining
At CTCA, we are proud to offer you and your caregiver health-conscious menus that feature delicious and nutritious meals at affordable prices. Our chefs work closely with our registered dietitians to develop a variety of dining options using the freshest ingredients, including:

  • Organic produce
  • Whole grains
  • Hormone-free meats (including chicken and beef)
  • Fresh fish
  • Sugar-free options
  • Gluten-free options

Activities and Classes
To help you pass the time between appointments and consultations, we offer patients and caregivers a variety of activities, such as New Friends Connection, Night Sky Tours and Drumming Circles.

Cancer Fighters® Program
The Cancer Fighters® program is a great way to stay connected to fellow CTCA patients and caregivers while at the hospital and at home. As a Cancer Fighters member, you will receive helpful communications that may include education from our clinical experts and updates on upcoming events at the hospital and in your hometown. Be sure to stop by the Cancer Fighters Corner located in the dining room while you are here.

New Section

To prepare for your evaluation, your medical oncologist reviews your medical records and notes from your Nurse Navigator. Your clinical situation is also presented to an integrative team that represents different areas of expertise. This will ensure that your needs are met with the various modalities and that your visit is productive.
