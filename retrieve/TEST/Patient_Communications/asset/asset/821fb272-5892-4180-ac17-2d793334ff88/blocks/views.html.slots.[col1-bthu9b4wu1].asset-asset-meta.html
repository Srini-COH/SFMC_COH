Visiting for your evaluation

You will meet your care team, learn about our services and receive your individualized treatment plan.

Your Evaluation

On your first day, you will meet with members of your care team to determine the diagnostic testing needed to confirm the results noted in your medical records. These tests, and any additional appointments with specialists, will be conducted throughout your visit. This information will help your care team develop a treatment plan personalized to your individual cancer type and needs.

Once all of the testing has been completed and your personalized treatment plan created, you will meet with your care team to discuss treatment options. During this discussion, you will have the opportunity to ask any questions you may have about your options so that you can choose the treatment plan that works best for you. At this time, you can also decide when you want to begin treatment.

Amenities

Concierge Service
Our Concierge Services team is available to you and your caregiver should you need assistance with access to maps and directions, train and shuttle schedules, activity schedules, fitness facilities and general information about the local area. This team can also help you plan trips around the Chicago or Milwaukee area. The concierge service is located in the front lobby at the main entrance.

On-Site Services
CTCA offers a variety of services and amenities on-site to make your trip here as convenient as possible, including:

  • Salon and Spa
  • Activity rooms with puzzles, games, computers, TV, foosball table and video game, small children’s play areas
  • On-site pharmacy and gift shop
  • Resource center with work stations

Dining
At CTCA, we are proud to offer you and your caregiver health-conscious menus that feature delicious and nutritious meals at affordable prices. Our executive chefs work closely with our registered dietitians to develop a variety of dining options using the freshest ingredients, including:

  • Organic produce
  • Whole grains
  • Hormone-free meats, including chicken and beef
  • Fresh fish
  • Sugar-free options
  • Gluten-free options

You may also select from a variety of “self-serve” and "made-to-order” dining options, including:

  • Expansive salad bar
  • Homemade soups
  • Fresh pastas
  • Grilled selections including fresh stir fries and burgers
  • Panini and deli sandwiches
  • Rotisserie specialties
  • Oven-baked wood-stone pizza
  • Homemade baked goods

Activities and Other Services
We offer a variety of activities you and your caregiver can participate in, such as bingo, craft classes and support groups. You can also attend planned outings around the Chicago or Milwaukee area, including sporting events, shopping tours, excursions to landmarks and special performances.

Cancer Fighters® Program
The Cancer Fighters® program is a great way to stay connected to fellow CTCA patients and caregivers while at the hospital and back home. As a Cancer Fighters member, you will receive updates that may include education from our clinical experts and upcoming events at the hospital. Be sure to stop by the Cancer Fighters Corner located in the lobby while you are here!

New Section

To prepare for your evaluation, your medical oncologist reviews your medical records and notes from your Nurse Navigator. Your clinical situation is also presented to an integrative team that represents different areas of expertise. This will ensure that your needs are met with the various modalities and that your visit is productive.
