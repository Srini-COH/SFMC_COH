Understanding our difference

Learn how integrative care helps patients feel better during treatment

Now that you have scheduled your appointment at CTCA, this short video will help you understand the benefits of our integrated approach to cancer care.

For example, you may want to know, how will your treatment at CTCA differ from the treatment you would receive elsewhere? Or, how can you feel better while fighting cancer?

Our hope is that this video will help answer some of those questions.

It’s important to recognize that treating cancer with state-of-the-art tools is only one step in the fight. The disease and its treatment may also take a toll on your body, mind and spirit.

That’s why the comprehensive treatment plan you will receive following your evaluation at CTCA will include both conventional cancer treatments (such as surgery, radiation and chemotherapy) and evidence-informed supportive therapies (such as nutritional support, physical therapy and naturopathic therapy).

These supportive therapies may help you manage possible side effects (like fatigue, nausea and pain), avoid delays in treatment and maintain your quality of life during treatment.

We call this approach integrative cancer care. It is designed to help patients feel better during treatment and get back to life quicker.

Please watch the video to learn more about what to expect and how your treatment at CTCA is likely to differ from the treatment you would receive elsewhere: Video: Preparing for Treatment

Amenities

On-Site Services
CTCA offers a variety of services and amenities on-site to help make your trip here as convenient as possible. These include:

  • Lily Bella Spa and Salon
  • Activity rooms with puzzles, games, computers, a big-screen TV, a pool table and sewing machines
  • On-site pharmacy and gift shop
  • Computers and work stations throughout the hospital
  • Library
  • Fitness Center
  • Chapel

Dining
At CTCA, licensed and registered dietitians work closely with the culinary team to develop high quality, health-conscious menus that feature delicious and nutritious meals at affordable prices.

Everything in our hospital kitchen is prepared with fresh ingredients, including:

  • A wide variety of seasonal produce
  • Whole grains
  • Lean proteins, such as chicken, turkey, beef and wild-caught fish
  • Diabetic friendly
  • Gluten-free
  • Low-fat

We know that cancer treatment may affect your appetite and food preferences, so let us know if you would like something special. We will make every effort to accommodate your requests and needs.

Activities and Other Services
We understand that it can be difficult to be away from home during cancer treatment. To make your visit as pleasant as possible, CTCA offers many activities and classes and in the hospital for patients and caregivers.

In addition, the Cancer Fighters® program is a great way to stay connected to fellow CTCA patients and caregivers while at the hospital or back home. As a member, you will receive ongoing communication, including education from our clinical experts and upcoming events onsite and in your hometown. Be sure to visit the Cancer Fighters Corner inside the Dining Room to learn more.

Activities and classes may include:

  • Patient and caregiver support groups
  • Game and movie nights
  • Educational classes
  • Cooking classes
  • Craft classes
  • Live music

Other services include:

  • Certified Medical Translator for those who speak English as a second language
  • Patient Relations Department to assist you with any special needs or concerns (a representative will meet you the first day; they can be reached at 918-286-5108)
