Traveling to CTCA

We strive to make traveling to our hospital easy and affordable for you and a travel companion.

Transportation

By Air
We are pleased to assist you with your transportation arrangements to and from the gateway airports that serve CTCA Phoenix. 

By Car
If you plan to drive to CTCA Phoenix, we can provide you with directions from your location or you can map directions yourself.

Around Town – Shuttle Service
The CTCA shuttle route and schedule will be posted in the lobby of the hospital and at our partnership hotels.

Lodging and Accommodations

Our goal is to provide convenient, comfortable and affordable accommodations for all CTCA patients, whether you stay onsite or at one of two nearby hotels. Our hospital offers 30 guest rooms onsite, which are reserved first. Once these rooms are full, we offer accommodations at nearby hotels with which CTCA has partnered. These partnership hotels offer discounted* rates that include complimentary breakfasts.

RV Accommodations
A special area of the parking lot is designated for RVs at CTCA Phoenix. Guests who wish to stay in their RV receive the customary amenities, including electricity, water and plumbing hook-ups, for $20 per night. 

* If you are a beneficiary of a government health insurance program, such as Medicare, CTCA is not permitted to provide travel, transportation or lodging benefits, including discounted hotel rates. However, we will do everything we can to assist you in coordinating your travel and finding affordable lodging.
