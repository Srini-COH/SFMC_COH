At our Outpatient Care Centers in North Phoenix and Scottsdale , you can meet with a cancer specialist (oncologist) for a one-hour consultation. The oncologist will review your medical records, answer your questions and work with you to develop a treatment plan based on your individual cancer diagnosis and needs. Appointments are available in as little as 24 hours.
