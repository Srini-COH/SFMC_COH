Patients and Donors Celebrate at BMT Reunion

City of Hope's 47th Annual Bone Marrow Transplant Reunion took place in October and featured an emotional meeting between patients and their donors. Known as the BMT Reunion Celebration of Life, this event is an annual highlight where patients reunite, rejoice and reminisce in their common bond as survivors.
