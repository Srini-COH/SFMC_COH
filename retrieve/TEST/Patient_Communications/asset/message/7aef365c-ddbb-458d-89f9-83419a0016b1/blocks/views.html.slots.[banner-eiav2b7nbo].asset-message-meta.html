Updated COVID-19 and Flu Vaccines Encouraged

Following CDC guidelines, City of Hope recommends both the updated fall 2023 COVID-19 and flu vaccine for all patients, caregivers and family members (ages 6 months and older). The COVID-19 vaccine's protection against severe illness and hospitalization is especially important for people with a weakened immune system, such as cancer patients. The flu vaccine can reduce flu illnesses and flu-related hospitalizations and deaths. You can receive the COVID-19 and flu vaccines together or separately; please discuss your choice with your doctor to ensure what's best for your current health status. For adults who are 60 years in age or older, consider also getting the RSV vaccine to help protect against infection by respiratory syncytial virus.
