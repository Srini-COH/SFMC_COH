Have you wondered if your genetic makeup puts you at a higher risk for cancer? What about for the rest of your family?

City of Hope is using genomic-driven precision medicine (treatment created based on genetic information) to treat cancer. If you have been seen at City of Hope for care in the past three years, you are invited to take part in our study to advance the science of cancer genetics.

The study provides no-cost comprehensive genetic testing to each City of Hope patient who has been seen for care in the past three years who opts in — regardless of whether they have had cancer or not.

Upon joining the study, you can opt into genetic testing for 188 genes linked to the hereditary risk for developing cancer and other diseases for which treatments exist. Information from this study may help our clinical care team create the best care and preventive plan for you and potentially for your family members. Our goal is to create lifesaving new therapies for future generations.

Participation is as easy as completing a simple at-home saliva kit. Get more information below to see if you’re eligible.
