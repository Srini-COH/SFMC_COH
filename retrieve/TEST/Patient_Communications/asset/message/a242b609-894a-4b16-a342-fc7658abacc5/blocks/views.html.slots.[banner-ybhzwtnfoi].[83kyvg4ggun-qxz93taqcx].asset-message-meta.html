Survivor Faces New Hurdle: Type 3 Diabetes

Following treatment for pancreatic cancer, Michael R.'s type 2 diabetes took on a new form calling for specialized care. For Michael, it wasn’t a total surprise since his father had died of pancreatic cancer 20 years earlier. Michael carries the BRCA2 genetic mutation, which can indicate a high risk for the disease.
