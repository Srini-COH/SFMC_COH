National Doctors' Day is on March 30. To celebrate, you're invited to share a note of appreciation for your doctor or doctors at City of Hope. Send it to the Cancer Fighters® team and we'll make sure your note reaches your doctor.

Interested in participating? You can choose any of these methods:

1. Mail in a letter. Write or print a typed letter of gratitude, send a quick postcard, a thank-you greeting card or anything you'd like to:
 
Cancer Fighters
2520 Elisha Avenue
Zion, IL 60099

2. Email a letter. Download the Doctors' Day Template below, fill it out and email it to CancerFighters@ctca-hope.com.

3. Email a photo or video. Share a photo of you, or one of you with your doctor, along with a few words about what your doctor mean to you.

4. Post on social media. Tell your followers about your doctor on social media. Tag us in your post for a chance to be featured on our channels.

All submissions must be received by Friday, March 24, to guarantee their delivery on National Doctors' Day.
