  • Open Enrollment Season is here and you have questions. Hours are spent pouring over information, reading through confusing tongue-twisters, and trying to decide the best options for you and your family.  The CTCA Business Services Team has compiled the following questions you might want to ask your employer’s benefits office as you make your open enrollment decisions.
  1. What type of plan is it? It’s best to choose a managed care plan and not an indemnity plan. When choosing a managed health care plan, it’s better to choose a PPO with OON benefits
  2. What are the maximum out of pocket costs associated with both in-network providers and out of network providers?
  3. Does this plan have benefits at CTCA? Are there any specific exclusions? Specialty Pharmacy (We don’t purchase drugs from specialty pharmacies)?
  4. Does this plan have cancer coverage? Chemotherapy, Radiation, etc?
  5. Are there daily, yearly or lifetime maximum’s associated with this plan?
