PROFESSIONAL:   The fusion of wellness and healthcare is what drives me to help others lead healthy lifestyles through individualized care practices. I earned my Master’s in Healthcare Administration with a specialization in strategic and innovative change along with a Bachelors in Kinesiology (the study of body movement). I have over nine years of health care experience, most recently, as Program Manager of Neurology (academic) and seven years as Manager of Wellness Services for a senior living center. I joined CTCA because, when my own family was affected by cancer, I was inspired by the compassion of their caregivers. I want to “give back” to people in the cancer community just as others have done for my family.
