As you begin your cancer care ministry, you have the opportunity to share the love of Christ with those in need, nurturing their faith and keeping their spirits high.
 
It is likely that you have witnessed firsthand the impact faith can have on a person's health; but did you know there is scientific evidence that supports this link between spirituality and health? We have gathered some of that information to help you in your cancer care ministry.
 
Visit Our Journey of Hope to find a resource called "Scientific Research and Spirituality." This document highlights some connections that various studies have found between people's health and their spirituality.
 
For example, in The Healing Power of Prayer, Dr. Harold Koenig of Duke University states that people with strong faith who suffer from physical illness have significantly better health outcomes than less religious people. Powerful insights like this can help inspire the members of your cancer care ministry by reinforcing the lasting impact a relationship with Christ can have on the overall health to those they are ministering.
 
Once you have determined your start date for small group training, please call or email me. I would like to send you additional materials to support your efforts. If you have any further questions or concerns, please feel free to contact me. I am happy to help. Thank you for your commitment to cancer patients.
