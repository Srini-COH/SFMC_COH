Despite disparities in health care quality, outcomes, complications, excess disease burden and premature death rates in cancer (in addition to many other illnesses) being well-documented, little progress has been made in reducing them. Even when physicians and health professionals are aware of these disparities, they may be unaware of how to handle these disparities when it comes to their own patients. 

In this series, the faculty will discuss the health disparities within cancer care, uncover some of the hurdles to providing better care, and expound on how health systems, communities and larger health care ecosystem can empower patients, providers, payors and others to build trust, provide access and use evidence-based practices to help advance health equity.
