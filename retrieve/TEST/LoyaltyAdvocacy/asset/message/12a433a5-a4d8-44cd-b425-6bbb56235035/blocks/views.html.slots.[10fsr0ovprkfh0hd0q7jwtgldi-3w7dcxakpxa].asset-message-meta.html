Visit CancerFighters.com and log in to get started. Don't have your online account yet? Contact us to get set up and save these instructions for later.

Once you've successfully logged in, follow these 3 steps to access your leaf:
  1. Navigate to My Profile by clicking the drop-down menu at the top right
  2. Click View My Leaf, appears midway down the screen
  3. Use the Update button to view, add and make changes
If you have already started adding to My Leaf, that's great! Be sure to continue answering the questions about your journey to ensure you've shared everything you want to have represented.

As part of the Celebrate Life event, your leaf will ceremoniously be added to the Survivor Tree that can be seen online and on the tree in the hospital. Learn more about this experience in the Survivor Tree FAQs.
