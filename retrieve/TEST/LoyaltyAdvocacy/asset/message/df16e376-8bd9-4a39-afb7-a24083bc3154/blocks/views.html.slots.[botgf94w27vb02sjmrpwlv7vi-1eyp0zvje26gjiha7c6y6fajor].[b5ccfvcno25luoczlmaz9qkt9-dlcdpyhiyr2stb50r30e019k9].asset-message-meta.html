Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) together with Cancer Fighters® is celebrating National Doctor's Day on Tuesday, March 30th.

On this day, we will recognize the contributions of physicians to individual lives and communities.

The patient is at the center of everything they do, so there's no better gift than to hear from the ones they join in the fight with every single day. 

Would you like to recognize your CTCA® doctor by sharing a heartfelt thank you video or letter? You can just follow these 3 easy steps to participate:
  1. Record a short video or write a gratitude letter recognizing your CTCA doctor. It can be a video simply thanking your doctor, or a special story you would love to share!
  2. Submit your video or letter by emailing cancerfighter@ctca-hope.com by Tuesday, March 23, 2021.
  3. Include in your email to Cancer Fighters your full name, the doctor's name(s) you are recognizing, and the facility where you treat. 
We will share your videos and notes with your doctor on March 30! 

Thank you for helping us celebrate in such a thoughtful way, and more importantly thank you for being the reason we celebrate in the first place.

Questions? Contact us at cancerfighter@ctca-hope.com.

Sincerely,
Your Cancer Fighters Team
