Since you are a valued member of Cancer Fighters® Care Net (CFCN), we want to share the exciting announcement of the newly established Office of the Chairman at Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA). Our Founders, the Stephenson family, together with the National Board of Directors recently established the Office of the Chairman, led by Darren Keller, Vice Chairman of the CTCA® National Board of Directors, to lead the strategic direction of CTCA. 

Joining Darren as members of the Office of The Chairman are:  Adesh Ramchandran, as Chief Operating Officer and Interim President, CTCA®, Anne Meisner, CEO of Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) Atlanta Chief Executive Officer, CTCA Atlanta and Chief Hospital Operations Officer, CTCA, and Dr. Maurie Markman, President of Medicine & Science, CTCA. This diverse and experienced team will assure that any initiatives CTCA undertakes are considered carefully with respect to the clinical, cultural and business impact on the patients and families we serve.  

Our strategic vision has not changed.  Our commitment to the Mother Standard® of care has never wavered and never will.  We will continue to look for opportunities to allow more cancer patients to be treated at CTCA with exceptional, patient-focused clinical services and treatment options.
