We are very pleased to announce that Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) is now officially part of City of Hope®, a National Cancer Institute-designated organization.

This joining of forces unites our shared vision, values and commitment to broaden access to high quality, compassionate care that puts patients first. As part of City of Hope, CTCA® will be able to expand your access to world-class cancer care, clinical trials, ground-breaking research and innovations in cancer treatments.

We want to assure you that your existing care remains unchanged. Your physicians and care teams, care location, scheduled appointments and support services will all be here to continue supporting you through your journey, and you will experience the same Mother Standard® of care that you deserve.
