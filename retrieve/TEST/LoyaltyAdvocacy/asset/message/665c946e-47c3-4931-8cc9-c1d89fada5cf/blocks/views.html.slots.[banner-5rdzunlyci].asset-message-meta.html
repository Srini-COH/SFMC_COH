Thank you for your continued support in our fight to give more Georgians access to the cancer care they want and deserve. I want to update you on House Bill 198, a certificate of need reform bill which includes removing our restrictions and much more.

Last Thursday, March 7, marked Day 28 of Georgia Legislative Session during which Rep. Matt Hatchett, champion of HB 198, presented the bill to the Georgia House of Representatives for a floor vote. After much spirited debate, HB 198 did not receive enough votes to pass over to the Senate.

While that is disappointing for many, it is important you hear this -- we are still in the game. The legislative process is complex, but we have a path forward, a game plan, legislative options, and importantly, the time and will to change the law that restricts Georgians from our care.
