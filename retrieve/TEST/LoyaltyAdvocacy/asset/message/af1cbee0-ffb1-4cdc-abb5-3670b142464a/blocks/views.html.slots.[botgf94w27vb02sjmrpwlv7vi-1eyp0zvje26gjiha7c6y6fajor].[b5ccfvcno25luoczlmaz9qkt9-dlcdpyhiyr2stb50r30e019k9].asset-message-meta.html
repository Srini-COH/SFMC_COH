Did you know that you can get involved with Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) in your community? Check out these opportunities to join with local cancer support organizations:

 
Relay for Life of Coweta County
 
Friday, September 16 from 6:00 p.m. – 10:00 p.m.
Crossroads Church, 3260 GA-16, Sharpsburg, GA 30277
 
Join Team CTCA® as we meet during Relay for Life of Coweta County. Register here online!

You're also invited to dedicate a Luminaria in honor of a loved one. At the end of the Relay, illuminated luminarias will line the Survivor Walk for all to see. Luminarias are $10 each, and all funds go directly to Relay for Life.

 
Georgia Ovarian Cancer Alliance Teal Trot 5k Walk & Run
 
Saturday, September 17 at 9:30 a.m.
Chastain BLUE Parking Lot, 4500 Stella Drive NW, Atlanta, GA 30342
 
Recognize Ovarian Cancer Awareness Month by joining CTCA and the Georgia Ovarian Cancer Alliance for their annual Teal Trot 5k Walk & Run. Ovarian cancer patients and survivors may use the promo code SURVIVOR during registration to participate at no cost. Register here online!

 
2022 Komen Georgia MORE THAN PINK Walk
 
Saturday, September 24 at 7:00 a.m. (check-in) with 9:00 a.m. start time
Lenox Square, 3393 Peachtree Road NE, Atlanta, GA 30326
 
Join CTCA and Susan G. Komen Georgia for their annual MORE THAN PINK Walk. Register here online!

If you've registered for any or all of the above events, be sure to email cfatlanta@ctca-hope.com with your name along with the number of guests that will be attending with you and your T-shirt size.
