Featured Speaker: Dr. Pamela Crilley: Dr.Crilley, DO is board certified in medical oncology and internal medicine. She earned an osteopathic medical degree from the Philadelphia College of Osteopathic Medicine. Dr. Crilley serves as a Hematologist-Oncologist and Medical Oncologist at CTCA. She says the integrated, team approach to caring for cancer patients has proven to be a unique and exciting opportunity. Her approach to patient care is first to listen to the patient, and then to try to provide hope, compassion and an innovative, evidence-based treatment plan with input from the team.
