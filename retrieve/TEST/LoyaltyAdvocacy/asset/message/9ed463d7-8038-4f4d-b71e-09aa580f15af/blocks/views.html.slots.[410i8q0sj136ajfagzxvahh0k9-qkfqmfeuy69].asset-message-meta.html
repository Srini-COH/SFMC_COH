Here's how to get started: Visit CancerFighters.com and log in to your account. From there, you can navigate to My Leaf and answer the prompts as they pertain to your experience.

If you don't have an online account set up yet, please email us and save the following instructions for later.

Once you're successfully logged in, follow these 3 steps to access your leaf:
  1. Navigate to My Profile by clicking the drop-down menu at the top right
  2. Click My Leaf, appears midway down the screen
  3. Use the Update button to view, add and make changes 
If you have already started adding to My Leaf, that's great! Be sure to continue answering the questions about your journey to ensure you've shared everything you want to have represented.

Do you have questions about the Celebrate Life event? Do you still need to RSVP to attend? Learn more by visiting CancerFighters.com/Celebrant.

We're thrilled to celebrate you and your incredible milestone soon!
