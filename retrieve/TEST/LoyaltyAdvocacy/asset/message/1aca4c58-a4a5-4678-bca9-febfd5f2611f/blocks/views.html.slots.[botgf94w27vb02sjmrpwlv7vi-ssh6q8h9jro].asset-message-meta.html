CTCA Conversion to Nonprofit Organization
On October 1, 2022, CTCA officially began operating as a nonprofit organization and submitted applications to the IRS to be recognized as a tax-exempt 501(c)(3) organization. Converting to a nonprofit allows us to establish philanthropic efforts directly supporting patient care and research activities and increase the impact of philanthropy for the overall City of Hope system. Learn more about our financial assistance policies at CTCA Atlanta, Chicago and Phoenix.
