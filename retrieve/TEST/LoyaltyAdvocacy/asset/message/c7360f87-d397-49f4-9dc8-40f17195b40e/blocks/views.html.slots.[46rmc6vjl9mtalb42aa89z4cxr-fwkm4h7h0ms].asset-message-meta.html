Target Audience:

This program is designed to meet the educational needs of primary care providers and other specialists involved in the care of patients with cancer.

Series Learning Objectives:

Upon completion of these educational activities, participants will be better able to: 

  • Discuss the impact of cardio-onocology on caring for patients with cancer
  • Discuss the utilization of palliative care in the management of patients with cancer
  • Discuss the utilization of genomic data in patients with cancer
