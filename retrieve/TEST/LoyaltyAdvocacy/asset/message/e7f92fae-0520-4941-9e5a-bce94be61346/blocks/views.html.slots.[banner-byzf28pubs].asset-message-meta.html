During these uncertain times, we have all been finding comfort in different ways, from completing puzzles and preparing home-cooked meals to finding new ways to connect with family and friends. Of course, there are many ways to connect electronically, but there is one simple way to brighten anyone's spirit — a handwritten card or letter!

You may remember that Cancer Fighters® offered a set of HOPE cards in March 2020. This exclusive set included five colorful cards, each with a unique uplifting message. We encouraged members to "make someone smile with these messages of hope, support and inspirations", but little did we know just how much support and inspiration would be needed in the months to come.

In an effort to continue spreading kindness and inspiration during this time, Cancer Fighters is pleased to again offer this HOPE card set to you!
