We have wonderful news to share! Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA), Chicago is pleased to announce that as of March 15, 2020 we are an in-network provider with Aetna. This new agreement, along with our existing relationships, allows CTCA® Chicago and both Illinois Outpatient Care Centers located in downtown Chicago and Gurnee, Illinois to expand its footprint of in-network care delivery for more patients and families in need of expert cancer care.

“Patients want and deserve choice and options in health care, and certainly when making decisions about cancer care,” said Pete Govorchin, President and CEO, CTCA Chicago. “The addition of Aetna as a contracted insurance provider will allow Aetna enrollees access to the high-quality clinical care and patient empowerment that are the very hallmarks of CTCA.”

This latest agreement with Aetna adds to contracts already in place at CTCA Chicago and allows CTCA to expand its footprint of in-network care delivery to patients and families in need of expert cancer care. The in-network options offered through Aetna include, but are not limited to: HMO, PPO, EPO, POS, QPOS, Elect Choice, Open Choice, Managed Choice POS, Aetna Choice POS II, Aetna Select, Aetna Student Health, indemnity plans with network incentives, Aetna Signature Administrators®, Joint Claims Administration, Meritain/Meritain Shared Administrative Services, Passport to Healthcare® and National Advantage Program. 

If you have a friend or loved one that may benefit from this new in-network agreement with Aetna or would just like to learn more about CTCA Chicago, please contact an Oncology Information Specialist at (844) 97 FIGHT (34448).
