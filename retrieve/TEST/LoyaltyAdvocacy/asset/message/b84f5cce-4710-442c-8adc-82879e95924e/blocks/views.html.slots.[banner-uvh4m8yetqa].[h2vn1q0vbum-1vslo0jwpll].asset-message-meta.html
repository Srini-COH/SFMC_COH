PROFESSIONAL: I have worked at CTCA for 30 years. I started in human resources just about 3 months after the Tulsa center opened in 1990. I transitioned over to speaking with patients in 1992. Helping others gives me great joy and I feel honored and blessed to help so many find the care they so deserve.

PERSONAL:  I am married to my husband, Karl, of 30 years. We have two grown children, a daughter and son, both in the Air Force. Our daughter is also married to a Submariner with the Navy and they have two boys, ages 7 and 5. Our son is single. I love being a grandmother and take every chance I get to go visit them.

VOLUNTEER WORK:  I am a member of the Assistance in Healthcare, Inc. Tulsa branch. This organization assists patients financially who are receiving cancer treatment in the Tulsa area for things like food, childcare, gas, housing, etc. I am also a member of the Hope Works volunteer group at CTCA.

HOBBIES: My husband and I are avid motorcyclists and we like to go on long trips. So far, the only state in the lower 48 not visited by motorcycle is Oregon. We have also traveled to many of the Canadian providences. We love to waterski and snow ski/snowboard and do our best to fit in these activities, too. The best times are when we coordinate the family time with the activities.
