Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) Chicago is excited to announce a new collaboration with Iroquois Memorial Hospital (IMH) located in Watseka, IL and serving patients in central Illinois and west central Indiana. Through this relationship, patients will receive coordinated care from both IMH and CTCA® clinicians through a combination of clinical on-site visits and online consultations (telemedicine), IMH cancer patients now have access to CTCA oncologists and advanced treatment options.

IMH will be hosting an open house to celebrate this new collaboration and to explain how the program works.
