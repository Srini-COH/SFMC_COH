With the advent of better imaging and screening programs, the rate of incidentaloma discovery has increased. The COVID-19 pandemic resulted in additional imaging studies being performed, discovering additional incidentalomas. PCPs are tasked with the management and/or care coordination of incidentalomas. The initial management of pulmonary incidentalomas requires effective collaboration.
