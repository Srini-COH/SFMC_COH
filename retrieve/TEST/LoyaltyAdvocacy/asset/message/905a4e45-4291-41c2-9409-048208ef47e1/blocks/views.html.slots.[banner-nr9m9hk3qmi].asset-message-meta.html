As you likely know, a cancer diagnosis can be stressful to patients and those who love them. Inspiration and encouragement are more important than ever during this time. Many times, the Bible can provide just the right words to help someone get through a difficult day. Scripture verses are one of the purest forms of encouragement, simple and true.
 
As you begin to lead your small group through cancer care ministry training, I want to provide you with an article called, "Scripture Provides Encouragement." This article highlights a handful of Bible verses that may be inspiring for those suffering in your congregation.
 
This article is featured in Cancer Fighters Thrive® e-newsletter, which is a monthly online publication designed to inspire, inform and empower cancer patients and their friends and family.
