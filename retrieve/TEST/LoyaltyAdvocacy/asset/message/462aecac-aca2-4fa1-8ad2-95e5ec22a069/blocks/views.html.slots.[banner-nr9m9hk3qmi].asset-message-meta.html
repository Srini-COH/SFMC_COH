It is likely that you have witnessed firsthand the impact faith can have on a person's health; but did you know there is scientific evidence that supports this link between spirituality and health? There is, and we have gathered some of that information to help you in your cancer care ministry.
 
Simply visit Our Journey of Hope website to find a resource called "Scientific Research and Spirituality." This information outlines the connections between spirituality and scientific research.
 
For example, research published by Dr. Harold Koenig of Duke University in his book “The Healing Power of Prayer” states that people with strong faith who suffer from physical illness have significantly better health outcomes than less religious people. Powerful insights like this can help inspire the members of your cancer care ministry by reinforcing the lasting impact a relationship with Christ can have on the overall health to those they are ministering.
