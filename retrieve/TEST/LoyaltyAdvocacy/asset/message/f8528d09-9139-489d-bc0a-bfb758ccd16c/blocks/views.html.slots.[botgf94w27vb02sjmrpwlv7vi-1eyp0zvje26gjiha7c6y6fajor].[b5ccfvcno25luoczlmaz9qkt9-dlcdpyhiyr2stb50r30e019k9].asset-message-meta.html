The outpouring of love and support for all staff, patients and families affected by the news of the closing of the Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) in Tulsa hospital has been encouraging and much appreciated.

Many Cancer Fighters® have reached out inquiring about ways to bid farewell and celebrate what was shared through this journey together at CTCA® Tulsa. Though the building will be closed to outside visitors beginning June 1, 2021, let’s share the love virtually with a special slideshow of the faces that turned into family. 

Here's what you can do: 
  • Submit a photo of you holding an encouraging message to cancerfighters@ctca-hope.com by Wednesday, May 26, 2021. 
  • All submitted photos will be pulled together in a slideshow, and sent to the Tulsa staff over the final week.
Thank you for the memories shared together along the way. Things might look a little different, but Cancer Fighters hope to remain part of your journey. Be sure to join the Tulsa Group on CancerFighters.com to stay in touch with the friends you have met along the way.  

Questions? Email cancerfighters@ctca-hope.com
