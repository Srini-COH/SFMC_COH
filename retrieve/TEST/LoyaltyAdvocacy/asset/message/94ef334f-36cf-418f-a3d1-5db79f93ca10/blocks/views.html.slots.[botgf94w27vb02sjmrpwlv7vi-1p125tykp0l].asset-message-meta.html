October is one of the most recognized cancer awareness months for good reason—following skin cancer, breast cancer is the second most commonly diagnosed cancer among U.S. women, affecting one in eight women who live to the age of 80.

With so many affected, we’ve made it our focus in October’s Creating a Healthier You newsletter. In addition, this article, Questions to ask your doctor about breast cancer treatment, is a great resource for patients and their caregivers.
