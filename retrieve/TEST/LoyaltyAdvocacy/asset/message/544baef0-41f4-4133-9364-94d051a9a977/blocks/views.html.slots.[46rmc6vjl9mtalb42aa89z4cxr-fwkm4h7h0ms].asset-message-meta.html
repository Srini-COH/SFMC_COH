Target Audience:

This program is designed to meet the educational needs of primary care providers and other specialists involved in the care of patients with cancer.

Learning Objectives:

Upon completion of this educational activity, participants will be better able to: 

  • Discuss the utilization of interventional pulmonology approaches in the management of patients with cancer
  • Discuss the utilization of interventional gastroenterology approaches in the management of patients with cancer
  • Discuss the utilization of interventional pain approaches in the management of patients with cancer
