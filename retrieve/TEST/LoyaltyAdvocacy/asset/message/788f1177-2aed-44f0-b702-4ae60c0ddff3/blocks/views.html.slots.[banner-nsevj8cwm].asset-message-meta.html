Hello from your CTCA Phoenix Cancer Fighter team!  
  • Help us celebrate the Outpatient Care Center Open House in Scottsdale Arizona.  Thursday, April 18th from 5:00 – 8:00 pm, CTCA welcomes the community to the Cancer Treatment Centers of America Outpatient Care Center located at 9755 N. 90th Street, Suite A100 and B 130 in Scottsdale, Arizona.  In addition to meeting our CTCA Physicians Dr. Salma Saeed and Dr. Farshid Sadeghi, there will be tours, wine and heavy hors d’oeuvres.  For more information or to RSVP, contact Laurann.Bivins@ctca-clinic.com, or call 480-935-4200. 
Be watching for information about the Open Houses for our North Phoenix AZ and Gilbert AZ Centers soon. 
  • Cancer Fighters Corner. The Cancer Fighters Corner is your place to belong, relax and recharge. While you are on campus, stop by to connect with your Cancer Fighters Team, meet other patients and caregivers and participate in activities. In April, we will be creating spring pallot signs, sock bunnies, handmade Easter cards, painted tote bags and more! 
  • Cancer Fighters Craft and Dinner Night at Red Lion. Join us on Thursday April 11th and Thursday April 25th for our fun dinner and craft nights from 5:30 – 7:00 pm.  On the 11th, volunteer Jean will be helping us create ribbon Stampin’ Up greeting cards for Easter, Mother’s Day and general blank note cards while we enjoy a chili buffet.  On the 25th, we’re back with spring pallet signs and a baked potato bar.  
  • Patient and Caregiver Breakfast Meetup. Start your day with Cancer Fighters!   Each Tuesday and Thursday, join your Cancer Fighters Team for a free breakfast for our patients and caregivers who have appointments that day. Breakfast is from 7:30 – 8:30 pm in the CTCA Dining Room.  This is a relaxed time to enjoy breakfast and conversation with other guests before you start your treatment day.  Breakfast is provided as a gift from Cancer Fighters for those that have appointments the day of the breakfast. 
  • Cancer Fighters Family Community Table. Pull up a chair and join the Cancer Fighter family table in the dining room!  Whether you have a few minutes between appointments or are sitting down to relax over a meal, this Cancer Fighters® family table is a place where you belong.  Meet new people and enjoy great conversations with old friends.  With Cancer Fighters, no one needs to dine alone.     
  • CTCA Cancer Fighters Facebook Page. Cancer Fighter’s national Facebook page is a fun way to stay connected with CTCA patients and caregivers across the nation.  This Facebook page is also a great way to share CTCA with your family and friends.  Make sure you go to our national page, CTCA Cancer Fighters, and like the page.  Then share the page with your family and friends and ask them to like the page to get their regular dose of encouragement, inspiration and wellness resources.  Help us get to 7000 people connected to this great resource.
