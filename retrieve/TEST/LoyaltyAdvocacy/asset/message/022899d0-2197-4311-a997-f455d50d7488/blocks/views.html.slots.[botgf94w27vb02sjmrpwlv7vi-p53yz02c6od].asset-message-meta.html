Dear %%=v(@FirstName)=%%,

We would like to start off by saying thank you for being a valued member of Cancer Fighters® Care Net (CFCN). Now more than ever, your efforts in sharing your stories and connecting with other Cancer Fighters members are of the utmost importance in creating a community of Cancer Fighters who nurture, empower and engage with one another. 

Because of this, we have an important task for you. We would like to encourage you, your caregivers and family members to provide a short, one-minute video testimonial about your journey with cancer and Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA). We will plan to share these with other Cancer Fighters members, as well as new patients through our digital platforms including our social media pages and website. 

Does this sound like something you would be interested in? If so, follow these quick and simple instructions:
  1. Pull out your smart phone, and find the Camera app.
  2. Open the Camera app and scroll left until you see the word “Video”. 
  3. Once you have the “Video” portion of your Camera app open and are ready to film, have yourself or a family member click the red “Record” button. *Note: If you will be filming yourself, please turn the Camera around to face you by clicking the “Reverse Camera” button in the bottom right hand corner of your screen. 
  4. To stop recording, please press the red “Record” button, and your video will be automatically saved to your photos folder. 
  5. To access your photos folder and view your video, please click on the “Photos Square” on the bottom left hand side of your screen. 
  6. Once you have your video pulled up and ready to send, please click the “Export” button or the “Square with an Arrow” button at the bottom left hand side of your screen. 
  7. Click the mail icon to pull up a new email, and compose an email with your video attachment to be sent to the Cancer Fighters team at cfcn@ctca-hope.com. 

Have questions about filming your video? Please refer to this simple step-by-step visual guide or reach out to the Cancer Fighters team at cfcn@ctca-hope.com. 

A few helpful filming tips: 
  1. Make sure you are filming yourself in a well-lit, quiet location.
  2. Perform a few test runs! Have yourself, family member or friend film a few times to check the sound and visual quality prior to filming your testimonial. Be sure to have all of the important details you’d like to highlight in mind. One minute will go by quickly!
  3. Make sure to wear solid, dark colored clothing. Please refrain from wearing any hats or baseball caps that may block your face.
  4. If you will be filming yourself, be sure to hold the camera steady. It can even work well to prop your phone up using a table or chair while filming. 
We appreciate all that you do in creating a sense of camaraderie and hope in the Cancer Fighters Community, and we look forward to seeing your video testimonial! 

Sincerely,
Your Cancer Fighters Team
