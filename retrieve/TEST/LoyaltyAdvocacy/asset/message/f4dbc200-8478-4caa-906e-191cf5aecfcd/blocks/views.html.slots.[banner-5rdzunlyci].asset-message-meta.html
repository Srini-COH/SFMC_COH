Hospital Update

The Radiology department is undergoing an exciting improvement! We are upgrading our CT scanner which will shorten the scan time for patients. While we undergo the installation of our new CT scanner, there will be no interruption in patient scheduling or daily treatment options.

What to expect:

  • On August 29, a mobile CT scanner arrived onsite to ensure that patient care is not impacted during removal and installation. When you arrive for your CT scan, a member of the team will make sure you are escorted to the appropriate area.
  • From September 1 to September 17, removal of our old CT scanner and installation of our new scanner will occur. Expect to see a forklist onsite and parking spaces on the southwest side of the building blocked during this period of time.
  • Patients will resume receiving their CT scans in the Imaging department on September 17.
​You should not experience any change in your schedule during this time. Thank you in advance for your patience during this process.
