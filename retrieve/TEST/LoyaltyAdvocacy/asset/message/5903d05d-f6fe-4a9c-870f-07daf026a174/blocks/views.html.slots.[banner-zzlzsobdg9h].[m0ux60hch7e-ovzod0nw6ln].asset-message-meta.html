“I'm nervous to travel from home for cancer treatment.”
 
It can be daunting to travel away from home to seek cancer treatment. You may know firsthand what it feels like. In fact, nearly 70% of our patients travel to one of our hospitals from another state. Your CTCA team tries to lift some of the burden by gathering medical records, scheduling appointments and making travel and lodging arrangements so that your treatment is as convenient and stress-free as possible. This Why Travel for Cancer Treatment is a great resource to share when talking to others about traveling for cancer care.
