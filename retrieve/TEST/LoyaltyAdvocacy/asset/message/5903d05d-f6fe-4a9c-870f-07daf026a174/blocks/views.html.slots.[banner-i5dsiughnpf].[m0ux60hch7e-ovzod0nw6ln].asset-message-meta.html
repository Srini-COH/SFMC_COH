“I don’t know if my insurance is accepted.”
 
CTCA now accepts more insurance plans than ever, including many plans from Aetna®, Cigna®, Blue Cross®, United Healthcare®, Medicare and many others. Because of the complexity involved in how insurance plans are developed, the best way to understand coverage is to contact CTCA. A member of the CTCA Team is ready to assist your loved ones and acquaintances with timely insurance verification, including in-network and out-of-network benefits, deductible plan coverage percentages and co-pays. You can reach this team by calling or sharing 844-97-FIGHT. This Navigating Insurance  was created to help address these concerns.
