Connecting with others who have first-hand experience fighting cancer can be empowering. At City of Hope®, we have a large team of patient and caregiver volunteers who are here to speak with you. These volunteers are available to share their stories, answer your questions and offer you and your loved ones insight on their experience.

We call this team our Cancer Fighters® Care Network (CFCN), and they are one of the most valuable resources we can offer to you through your experience at City of Hope.
