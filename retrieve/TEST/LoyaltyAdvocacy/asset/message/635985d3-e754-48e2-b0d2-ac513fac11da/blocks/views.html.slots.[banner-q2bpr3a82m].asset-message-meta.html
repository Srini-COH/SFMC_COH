As you can imagine, faith can make a big difference in someone's life, especially when it comes to their perspective on life and their medical care. We have learned that according to a “Coping with Cancer Study” report published by ‘Dana Farber’ Cancer Institute and Harvard Medical School:
  • 88 percent of patients considered religion to be at least somewhat important. *
  • Approximately 40 percent of patients say their spiritual needs were largely or entirely unmet by a religious community. *
