Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) is now City of Hope®, uniting our shared vision, values and commitment to high-quality, compassionate care that puts patients first. We've achieved a number of milestones with the integration of CTCA®, including CTCA locations now functioning as nonprofit organizations, the ongoing implementation of City of Hope clinical and quality policies, and joint quality reviews and tumor boards to integrate findings and insight on complex cases encouraging the best care across all locations. City of Hope has also brought its world-renowned expertise in bone marrow and blood stem cell transplants and CAR-T cell therapy to the Phoenix and Chicago locations.

Now, as part of this ongoing transformation, we've reached another important milestone — the rebranding of CTCA locations to City of Hope Atlanta, City of Hope Chicago and City of Hope Phoenix.

As we maximize the reach and expertise of our national system, we are committed to keeping you up to date on the important progress taking place at City of Hope.
