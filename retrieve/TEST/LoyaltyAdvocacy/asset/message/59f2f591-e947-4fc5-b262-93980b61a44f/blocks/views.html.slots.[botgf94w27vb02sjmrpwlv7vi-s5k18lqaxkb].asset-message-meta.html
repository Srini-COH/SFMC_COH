City of Hope Physicians Collaborating Through Joint Tumor Boards
Currently, patient cases are being presented at joint tumor boards, where our experts provide education and insights on complex cases to provide the best care across all locations. This type of collaboration has enormous benefits for patients as it allows for partnership across the City of Hope national system.

For instance, City of Hope Chicago reached out to subspecialists at City of Hope Los Angeles regarding a rare genomic alteration for non-small lung cancer in the EGFR gene. Based on experience with this cancer and its treatment, City of Hope Los Angeles doctors knew it would have a limited effect and shared insights about an alternative course of treatment based on research conducted at City of Hope.
