Lorena is originally from Venezuela, where she obtained a degree in Family Medicine. After working as a physician for a few years, she moved to the U.S. She joined the CTCA family as a Patient Advocate in our Tulsa hospital in 2012 where she focused primarily on expanding our ability to cater to the Hispanic community. In 2014 she moved to the Midwest and transferred to our Zion hospital as a Patient Navigator. 

She considers herself a foodie and loves watching movies with her husband and two daughters, drawing, and listening to podcasts.
