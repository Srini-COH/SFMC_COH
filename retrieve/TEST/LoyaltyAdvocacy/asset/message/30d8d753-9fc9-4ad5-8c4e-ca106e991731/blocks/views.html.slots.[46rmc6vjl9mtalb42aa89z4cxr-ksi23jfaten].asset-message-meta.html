This program is designed to improve the knowledge base of oncology multidisciplinary teams about the expanding role and procedures of interventional endoscopy in cancer care with focus on gastrointestinal malignancies.

A panel of expert speakers will provide updates on guidelines in GI cancer screening, diagnosis, staging and palliation with special emphasis on the cancer population.
