PROFESSIONAL: I joined CTCA in 2010. I previously worked in the high-tech field helping companies like GE Medical build out and supply our nations hospitals with imaging systems like PET/CT and MRI rooms. Medicine and the human body have always been interesting to me.

PERSONAL:  I am a 5 time triple negative breast cancer survivor, having found breast cancer when I was 30 years old and 7 months pregnant. After many years of surgeries, testing, chemo and radiation, I found my true calling working at CTCA to speak with other cancer patients.

VOLUNTEER WORK:  I have worked for the Relay for Life and the Susan G Komen society for many years. I think fundraising is such a great way to give back and help those in need.

HOBBIES: I am an avid reader and love watching historical dramas. I am pretty good with DIY projects and have a full complement of tools and devices to make me pretty 'dangerous' around the house.
