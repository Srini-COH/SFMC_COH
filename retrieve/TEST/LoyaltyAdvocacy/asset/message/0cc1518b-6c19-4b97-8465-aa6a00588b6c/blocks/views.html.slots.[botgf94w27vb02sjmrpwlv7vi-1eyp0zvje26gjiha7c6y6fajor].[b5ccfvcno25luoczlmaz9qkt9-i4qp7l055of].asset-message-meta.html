CONNECT with the COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT AND OUTREACH TEAM (CEOT)

The Community Engagement and Outreach Team (CEOT) is available to you as a valued member of the Cancer Fighters program. CEOT offers your referrals concierge-level access to CTCA® with additional support, education, and guidance for seeking cancer treatment. Whether you are speaking to someone on the phone or striking up a conversation about your cancer journey with CTCA at a social event, CEOT is available to assist you by providing you with access to the following tools and resources:
  • A direct phone number for questions and referrals: 844-97-FIGHT
  • A team email at ceot@ctca-hope.com
This team will ensure that every person you speak to about CTCA receives accurate information about qualifying for treatment and expert, personalized service should he or she choose to treat at CTCA.
