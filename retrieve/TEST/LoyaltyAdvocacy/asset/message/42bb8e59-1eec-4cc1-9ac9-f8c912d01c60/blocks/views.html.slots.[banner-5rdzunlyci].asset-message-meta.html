Dear %%=v(@FirstName)=%%,

We are thrilled to introduce your Patient Experience Facilitator, Chequita Glover! Last year, our Cancer Fighters® referred 438 of their friends and family to Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) Atlanta for the quality care they deserve. To ensure your referrals receive the most personalized and seamless introduction to CTCA®, Chequita will now serve as a dedicated resource for your friends and family within our hospital. Just like our Oncology Information Specialists, Chequita will process insurance information and schedule appointments, as needed, with our specialized teams of experts.
 
Please join us in welcoming Chequita to the Cancer Fighters Team! We asked Chequita why she is so passionate about her role; check out her answers below!
 
Cancer Fighters: Why did you choose to work at CTCA? 
 
Chequita: I chose CTCA because my father passed away from lung cancer and I wanted to learn everything there is to know about this awful disease. 
 
Cancer Fighters: What do you like most about your job? 
 
Chequita: I am able to meet and help people every day. My patients teach me so much about life, love and family. Their entire world has been rocked by a diagnosis of cancer, and somehow they still seem to find strength to keep fighting.
