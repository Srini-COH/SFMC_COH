Have you ever found yourself feeling overwhelmed by the paperwork involved with navigating cancer treatment? Let's get organized!

Cancer Fighters partnered with patients, caregivers and employees at Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA), part of City of Hope, to create this customizable Cancer Care Organizer to help you get and stay organized.

The Cancer Care Organizer is a 25-page notebook that is sectioned into treatment-related categories. The sections can be customized to meet your needs and features:

  • 5 designated sections with individual folders
  • 36 section labels, including 16 blank labels
  • Bright, color-coded folders for easy reference
  • A built-in business card holder with 10 slots for easy viewing
