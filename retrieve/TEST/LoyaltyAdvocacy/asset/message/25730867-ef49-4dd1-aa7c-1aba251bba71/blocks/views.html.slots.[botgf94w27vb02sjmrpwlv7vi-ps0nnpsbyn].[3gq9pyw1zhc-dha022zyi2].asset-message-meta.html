Meet Your Moderator -- Katherine Anderson

Since joining CTCA® Tulsa in 2004, Anderson has provided clinical oversight to physical therapists, occupational therapists, speech language therapists, massage therapists, naturopathic providers, acupuncturists and therapy assistants. She is part of a team that delivers a personalized, integrative approach in a collaborative environment, all under one roof. 

Anderson also is involved in local community programs, such as the Colon Cancer Coalition, Susan G. Komen and Tulsa Regional STEM Alliance.
