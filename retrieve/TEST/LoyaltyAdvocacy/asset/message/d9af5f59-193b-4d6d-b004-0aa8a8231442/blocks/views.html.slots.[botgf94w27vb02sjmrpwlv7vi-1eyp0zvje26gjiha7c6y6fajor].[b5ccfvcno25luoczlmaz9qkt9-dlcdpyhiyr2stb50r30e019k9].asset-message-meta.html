Cancer can put a strain on relationships. Treatments for cancer and the disease itself may cause changes in your desire and function. If your physical appearance has changed, you may feel less attractive or desirable. At the same time, your partner may feel helpless, fearful or unsure of how to support you. Any of these issues can form barriers to intimacy.
