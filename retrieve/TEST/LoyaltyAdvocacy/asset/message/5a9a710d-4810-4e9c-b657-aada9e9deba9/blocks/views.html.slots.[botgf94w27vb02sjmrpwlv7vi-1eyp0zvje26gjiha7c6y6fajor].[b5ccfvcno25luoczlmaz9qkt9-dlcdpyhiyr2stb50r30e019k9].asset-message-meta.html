Dear %%=v(@FirstName)=%%,

As a valued Cancer Fighters® Care Net (CFCN) member, we are thrilled to share with you advanced access to a new Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) benefit – the myCTCA patient portal app, available for both iPhone and Android mobile phones and tablets. The new app has all the same features as the myCTCA.com website portal with plans to offer even more capabilities soon.  

With the myCTCA app, you can easily do the following:
  • View and manage your appointments
  • See your current medications
  • Request prescription renewals
  • Send a secure message to your care team
  • View your lab results and medical records
  • Join telehealth appointments
  • Share your records with caregivers and hometown physicians
  • Print schedules in the same easy-to-read format available at the hospitals
  • View customized graphs that show trends in vitals such as weight, blood pressure and patient-reported symptoms…and more!
Download the myCTCA app by visiting the Apple or Android app store. From there, enter your existing myCTCA login and password. If you are not a current myCTCA patient portal user, you can register by following the below steps:
  1. Once you’ve downloaded the myCTCA app, select “Don’t have an account? Register here.”
  2. Create your ID by filling out the requested information (name, email address and a password). Please note: Each account requires a unique email address.
  3. Click the check box, “I am not a robot” (You will need to select certain items in a picture to verify you are a real person).
  4. Choose three security questions to use to verify who you are.
  5. Enter your patient information (e.g., medical record number, last name, date of birth and ZIP code).
  6. You will be sent an email with a code. Enter the code provided to complete the registration process.
For help with log in, resetting passwords or other technical issues with the app, please call Technical Support at 877-448-1725.

We hope you will find the new app an enhancement to your medical information and experience at CTCA!

Sincerely,
Your Cancer Fighters Team
