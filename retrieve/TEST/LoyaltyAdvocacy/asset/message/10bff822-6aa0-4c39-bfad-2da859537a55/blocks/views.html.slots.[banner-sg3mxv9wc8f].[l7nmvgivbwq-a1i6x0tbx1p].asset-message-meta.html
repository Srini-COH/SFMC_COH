Leigh Ann loves the great outdoors and enjoys traveling. Long drives and sunny days are something she treasures.

She loves to spend time with family and friends, especially when watching football or playing golf with her dad. She is also a former professional golfer, having spent 15 years on the LPGA Tour.

In her spare time, Leigh Ann volunteers with a few organizations like The First Tee to help teach at-risk youth both golf and life skills.
