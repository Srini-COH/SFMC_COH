By now you should have received your invitation for this year’s Celebrate Life® event. We hope you can join us in celebrating this momentous occasion to honor you and fellow patients at Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA)!

Now that it’s your year to celebrate, you’re eligible to have your leaf on the Celebrate Life Survivor Tree—congratulations!

The Survivor Tree is a longstanding tradition at CTCA®, showing stories from patients who have reached the five-year anniversary from their first day of treatment.

Before your leaf can be added to the tree, let’s verify your information and get your story recorded using My Leaf. This online tool helps make it easier to celebrate you and your amazing milestone.

Ready to begin? Simply answer the different questions as they pertain to your experience, at your convenience. Visit CancerFighters.com and log in to get started. Don’t have your online account set up yet? Contact us to get set up and save these instructions for later.

Once you’re successfully logged in, follow these 3 steps to access your leaf:
  1. Navigate to My Profile by clicking the drop-down menu at the top right
  2. Click My Leaf, appears midway down the screen
  3. Use the Update button to view, add and make changes  
This September, as part of the Celebrate Life event, your leaf will ceremoniously be added to the Survivor Tree that can be seen online and on the tree in the hospital.

For more information, check out the My Leaf guide or email us CelebrateLife@ctca-hope.com with any questions.
