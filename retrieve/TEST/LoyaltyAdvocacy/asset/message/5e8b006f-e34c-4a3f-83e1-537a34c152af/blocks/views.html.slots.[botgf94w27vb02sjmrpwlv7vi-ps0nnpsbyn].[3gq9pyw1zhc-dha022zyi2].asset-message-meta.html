Meet Your Moderator:
Nejd Alsikafi, MD, FACS

Dr. Alsikafi serves patients who have prostate, bladder, kidney or testicular cancer. He consults with medical oncologists and patients on available treatment options, performs surgery and monitors patients who many need surgical intervention in the future.

Inspired to become a doctor because of his desire to help people, Dr. Alsikafi says, "My father was a physician who was an excellent role model. I chose urology specifically because it was a beautiful blend of surgery and medical management. It also dealt with sensitive topics, which I felt my personality was well suited to discuss with patients."
