Various studies have found a connection between people's health and their spirituality. In The Healing Power of Prayer, Dr. Harold Koenig of Duke University states that people with strong faith who suffer from physical illness have significantly better health outcomes than less religious people.

Powerful insights like this can help inspire the members of your cancer care ministry by reinforcing the lasting impact a relationship with Christ can have on the overall health to those they are ministering.
