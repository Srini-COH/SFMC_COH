Dear %%=v(@FirstName)=%%,

Cancer screening is one of the most powerful tools you have to reduce cancer risk or risk of recurrence as early detection saves lives. That’s why Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA), Atlanta invites you, your caregivers and loved ones to attend a special weekend lung screening event from Friday, August 27 through Sunday, August 29, 2021. 

Schedule your screening appointment by calling 770-400-6515.

Appointments are covered by most insurances, or are $225 without insurance coverage, and include:
  • A low-dose CT scan
  • Educational materials
  • Light snacks, refreshments and take-home goodie bags
To qualify for an appointment, you or your loved ones must meet the following lung screening criteria:
  • 50 years in age or older
  • Current smoker or have quit within the past 15 years
  • A 20-pack-year smoking history (one pack a day for 20 years, or two packs a day for 10 years)
Appointments are required; no walk-in screenings are allowed at this time. Please be prepared for a brief COVID-19 screening upon arrival. Masks are mandatory. Please email ashley.daniels@ctca-hope.com with any questions.
