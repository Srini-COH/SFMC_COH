Do you forget things you once remembered easily? Feel mentally foggy and have trouble concentrating or understanding what you read? Find it difficult to manage more than one task at a time?

If you answered yes to any of these questions, you may be struggling with cancer-related cognitive impairment, also known as "chemo brain."

The good news is, you can train your brain and mental focus capabilities with specific activities identified to help overcome these obstacles.

Cancer Fighters® teamed up with City of Hope® Atlanta Speech-Language Pathologist Jennifer Cargile, MEd, CCC-SLP, to bring you the Brain Trainer Activity Book from Cancer Fighters designed to help you cope with cognitive changes you may be experiencing.
