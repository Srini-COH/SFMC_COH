If you’d rather not be part of the group, you can remove yourself at any time. Once you log in, select My Profile by clicking the drop-down menu at the very top right. Scroll down and click on the name of the group you're looking to leave, listed at the bottom right. Click the group name and hover over where it says Member at the top right, then click when it changes to Leave. You can re-join at any time following the same process.
