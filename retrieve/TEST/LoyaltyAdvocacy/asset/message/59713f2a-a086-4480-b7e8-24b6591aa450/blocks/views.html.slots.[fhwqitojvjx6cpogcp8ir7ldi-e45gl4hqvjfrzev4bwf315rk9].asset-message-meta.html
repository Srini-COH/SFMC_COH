Cancer Fighters Corner Monthly Update
 
In celebration of all of cancer survivors that make up the Cancer Fighters community, our Cancer Fighters teams will be hosting celebratory on-site and virtual events for you to take part in all month long! 

Virtual offerings: Below are just a few highlights for the month of June! Be sure to visit the Events section online to register and view a full list of upcoming virtual activities.  

•    Writing Through it with Karla Baptiste
•    Open Mic Night
•    Men’s Virtual Support Group 

Did you miss a virtual session, and want to catch the replay? Visit YouTube.com/CancerFighters to watch and subscribe. 

On-site offerings: Heading to CTCA for an appointment this month? Check out the full list of what’s happening on-site. 
 
Online offerings: Earn your official survivor badge on cancerfighters.com. Log in any time during the month of June to receive the badge. Search for fellow Cancer Fighters in Connections. You can search for an individual or find new friends by applying the Journey or Cancer Type filter. Follow them and show them some love by giving them a badge.
