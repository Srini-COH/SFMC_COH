One in two cancer patients experience significant emotional distress.1 When you take the steps to relax your body and renew your mind, you may be on your way to feeling like yourself again.
 
Teresa Burnett, a board-certified music therapist at Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) has recorded a podcast therapy session – for Cancer Fighters® members – to help manage stress and relieve its distressing side effects. It’s called Relax & Renew: A guided music therapy experience.
