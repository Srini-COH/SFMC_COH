The Psychology of Survivorship


What is survivorship? What are the mental and emotional side effects that often come with a cancer diagnosis and how has life changed moving forward? Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) Chicago Behavioral Health Therapist Brian Larsen, LCPC, has spent many years working with patients to navigate and cope with this unimaginable life change. 

In this virtual presentation on the Psychology of Cancer Survivorship, Brian gives tools used to cope with all the life changes that may come with a cancer diagnosis, and addresses a range of topics.
