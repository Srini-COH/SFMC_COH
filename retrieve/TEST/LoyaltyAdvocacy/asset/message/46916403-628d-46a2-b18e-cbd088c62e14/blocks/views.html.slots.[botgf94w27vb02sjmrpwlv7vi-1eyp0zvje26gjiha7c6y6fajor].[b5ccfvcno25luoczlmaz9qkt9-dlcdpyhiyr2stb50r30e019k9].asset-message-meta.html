Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) and Cancer Fighters® is excited to introduce text message notifications. Alerts will now be sent straight to your phone each time you receive a new Cancer Fighters Care Net (CFCN) request! 

How will it work?
  • You will receive the following text message on Thursday, May 7. 
Welcome to SMS messages to alert you to new CFCN requests to contact patients considering treatment at CTCA. Msg&Data rates may apply. Frequency of messages per month may vary. Text HELP for Help or text STOP to end messages. View our Privacy Policy.
 
  • You will begin receiving a text message and an email each time you have a new CFCN request. The text message will read:
You have received a CFCN request to contact a patient considering treatment at CTCA. Please login to cancerfighter.com for details.

Please note: you will still be required to login to cancerfighter.com to securely receive your CFCN requests.

Not interested in receiving text messages? No problem! Simply respond "STOP" to the text on Thursday. You can always opt-in at a later date and time too!

If you have any questions or would like to update your mobile number, please contact Jen Mikes at cfcn@ctca-hope.com.
