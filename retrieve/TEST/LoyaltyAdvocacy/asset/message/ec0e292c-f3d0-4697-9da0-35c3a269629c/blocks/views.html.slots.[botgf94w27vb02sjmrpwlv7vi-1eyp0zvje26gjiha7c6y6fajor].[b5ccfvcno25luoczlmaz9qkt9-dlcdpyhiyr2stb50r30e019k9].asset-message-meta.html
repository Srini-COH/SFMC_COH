Why is Getting Good Sleep So Important? 


Do you have difficulty sleeping? You’re not alone. Individuals with cancer, and even their loved ones, often report higher rates of sleep disturbances than the general population.

In this article, Mike Uhl, Behavioral Health Therapist at CTCA® Chicago, shares his experience in working with cancer patients and tips to get you on your way to better quality sleep.

Mike covers topics like: 
  • Finding your ideal conditions for a good night’s sleep
  • Tips to consider as you’re nearing bedtime
  • Techniques to help go back to sleep if you find yourself awake at night
