The Georgia Renaissance Festival is celebrating their 36th anniversary and inviting all Cancer Fighters® members to join the festivities! 

You're eligible to claim two complimentary tickets to the festival by emailing us with your name and the name of the additional guest you wish to accompany you. Your tickets will be waiting for you on the day of your visit at the Box Office. Tickets are limited two tickets per household.

For more information about The Georgia Renaissance Festival, along with driving instructions, please visit garenfest.com. 

If you have any questions, please reach out to your Cancer Fighters Team at cfatlanta@ctca-hope.com.
