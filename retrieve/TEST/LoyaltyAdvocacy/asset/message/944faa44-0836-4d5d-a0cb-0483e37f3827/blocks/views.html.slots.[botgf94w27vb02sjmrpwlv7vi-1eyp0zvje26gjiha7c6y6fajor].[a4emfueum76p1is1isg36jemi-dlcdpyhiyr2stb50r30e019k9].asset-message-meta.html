Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) Atlanta is hosting a free, six-week support program for children ages 6-11 or in grades K-5 who currently have a parent or grandparent experiencing cancer.

This emotional support group is called CLIMB® and is part of The Children's Treehouse Foundation. In partnership with CTCA® Atlanta, specially trained staff will be meeting weekly to help children:
  • Learn more about cancer
  • Identify different coping skills
  • Reduce stress and improve communication
  • Have a safe space to talk about difficult feelings

Meeting every Monday from January 23 - February 27
