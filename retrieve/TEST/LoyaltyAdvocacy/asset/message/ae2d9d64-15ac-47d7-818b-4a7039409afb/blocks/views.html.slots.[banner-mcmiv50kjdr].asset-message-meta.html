Hello from your CTCA Phoenix Cancer Fighter team! 
  • Cancer Fighters Holiday Dinner: Join us on Thursday evening November 29th for the Cancer Fighters Holiday Dinner at the Pebblecreek Tuscany Ballroom. Current patients are invited to sign up for themselves and an adult caregiver for this dinner that starts with a cocktail mixer at 5:30 pm and then dinner at 6:00 pm. Guests will enjoy a delicious dinner of a bacon wrapped chicken and glazed salmon combination entrée plate with sides, salad and dessert.  All menu items are gluten free and a vegetarian plate is available by request.
Seating is limited and RSVP is required.  To sign-up, contact Cancer Fighters at CFWestern@ctca-hope.com or 623-207-3453.  
  • Cancer Fighters Family Community Table: Pull up a chair and join the new Cancer Fighter family table in the dining room!  Whether you have a few minutes between appointments or are sitting down to relax over a meal, this Cancer Fighters® family table is a place where you belong.  Meet new people and enjoy great conversations with old friends.  With Cancer Fighters, no one needs to dine alone.    
  • CTCA Cancer Fighters Facebook Page: Cancer Fighters national Facebook page is a fun way to stay connected with CTCA patients and caregivers across the nation. This Facebook page is also a great way to share CTCA with your family and friends.  Make sure you go to our national page, CTCA Cancer Fighters, and like the page. Then share the page with your family and friends and ask them to like the page to get their regular dose of encouragement, inspiration and wellness resources. Help us get to 7000 people connected to this great resource.
  • Cancer Fighters Corner: The Cancer Fighters Corner is your place to belong, relax and recharge. While you are on campus, stop by to connect with your Cancer Fighters Team, meet other patients and caregivers and participate in activities.  Throughout November, you will have the opportunity to put together Thanksgiving cards and get ready for the holidays.
