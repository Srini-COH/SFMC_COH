Experience the musical stylings of Tommie Macon and the Gentlemen of Jazz, hosted by The City of Newnan Cultural Arts Commission in partnership with Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA), Atlanta.

You're eligible to claim two seats and receive a special CTCA® goodie bag to enjoy during the event. Simply claim your spot by sending in your name and the name of your guest.

Be sure to stop by the CTCA tent during the event to get your goodie bag! Blankets and picnic baskets are welcome. Food trucks will be onsite.

We hope to see you there!
