Learn with LUNGevity and CTCA®
Wednesday, May 12, 2021 from 1:00 p.m. to 3:00 p.m. EST
Online – a Zoom link will be emailed upon registration

Join CTCA clinical experts Dr. Dan Miller and Dr. Herbert Duvivier as they share resources and information that lung cancer patients should know about advancements in minimally invasive surgical procedures as well as treatment options for surgically resected patients. Find out about survivorship and patient support programs offered by CTCA and the LUNGevity Foundation.

This event has no cost to attend and requires an online registration to hold your seat.
