Meet Your Moderator

As a Clinical Oncology Dietitian at CTCA, Melissa Picchietti creates individual nutrition plans based on each patient's medical history and individual needs. She is committed to helping patients maintain nutritional balance while undergoing cancer treatment, so they can reach their short- and long-term goals.   

Outside of CTCA, Picchietti enjoys spending time with her family and friends. She also likes drawing, painting, running, hiking and spending time outdoors.
