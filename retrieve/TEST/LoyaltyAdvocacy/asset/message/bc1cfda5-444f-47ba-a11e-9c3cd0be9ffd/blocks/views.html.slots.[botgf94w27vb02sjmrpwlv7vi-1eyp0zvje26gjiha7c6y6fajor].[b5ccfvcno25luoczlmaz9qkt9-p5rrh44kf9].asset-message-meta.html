Caregiving for cancer patients comes in many different forms. Many cancer patients are fortunate to have family and friends living nearby who can perform caregiving duties. But if your family lives far away or has full-time work and family commitments, they may not be able to assist with the care you need.

For some, having more than one caregiver that helps with various tasks is the best approach. In this article, When to hire a cancer caregiver, learn about how to find and hire the various types of caregivers along with ideas for what questions to ask when meeting with them.
