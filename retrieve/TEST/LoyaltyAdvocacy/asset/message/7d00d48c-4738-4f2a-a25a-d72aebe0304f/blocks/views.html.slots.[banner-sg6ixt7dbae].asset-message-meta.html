To use myCTCA.com you need to register. Just follow these six steps:

  1. Go to myCTCA.com and select “Don’t have a CTCA ID? Create one.”
  2. Create your ID by filling out the requested information (name, email address and password). Please note: Each account requires a unique email address.
  3. Click the check box, “I am not a robot” (You will need to select certain items in a picture to verify you are a real person).
  4. Choose three security questions to verify your identity.
  5. Enter your patient information (e.g. medical record number, last four digits of your social security number, date of birth and zip code).
  6. You will be sent an email and you will need to enter the code provided to complete the registration process.

If you registered previously, you will need to create a new, secure password. Then, simply log in to have all your vital CTCA information at your fingertips.
