Visit us at the Hospital. CTCA has a space dedicated at each hospital just for Cancer Fighters members. It is called the Cancer Fighters Corner. Cancer Fighters Corner is a great place to connect with other patients and caregivers, find out what’s happening onsite, and browse through the wall of resources. Be sure to stop in the next time you are onsite to learn more ways our team can support your caregiver. We are here to help! To learn more, email your team.
