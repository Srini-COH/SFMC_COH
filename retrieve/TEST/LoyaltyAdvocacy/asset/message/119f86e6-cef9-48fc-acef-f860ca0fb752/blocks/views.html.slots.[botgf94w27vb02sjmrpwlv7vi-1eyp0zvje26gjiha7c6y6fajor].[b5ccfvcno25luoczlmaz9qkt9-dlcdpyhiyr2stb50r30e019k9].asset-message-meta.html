Dear %%=v(@FirstName)=%%,

As the COVID-19 outbreak continues to evolve, you probably have lots of questions. But with so much information on the internet, some of it conflicting and misleading, you may find that getting reliable, credible answers can be challenging—especially when it comes to information geared specifically to you, the cancer patient. To help fill that void, we want to offer an easy and convenient way to have your questions answered by a leading clinical expert. 

Each week, on Thursdays at 3 p.m. Eastern, Dr. Maurie Markman, President of Medicine & Science at Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) and a world-renowned medical oncologist, hosts a live Twitter chat, answering cancer patients’ COVID-19-related questions. 
 
Recent questions have included:
  • Does my risk for contracting #COVID19 depend on the type of cancer I have?
  • I’ve heard reports about workers in meat-packing plants getting #COVID19. Do I have a higher risk of getting the virus as a cancer patient if I eat meat?
  • I’m taking immunotherapy to treat my cancer. Will this treatment protect me from getting #COVID19 because it’s using my immune system to fight the cancer?
Do you have any questions you want Dr. Markman to answer? All you have to do is submit them, either before Thursday or during the one-hour chat. You do have to have a Twitter account to participate during the chat, but you don’t have to have one to submit a question beforehand. 

To submit a question before the chat, ask it in the comments box of our Facebook or Twitter posts and use the hashtag: #AskDrMarkman. Then we’ll share it out during the chat. Don't have a social media account? Simply click here to submit your question and we will ask it in your name.

To follow along and/or participate in the chat, go to @CancerCenter on Twitter or follow the #AskDrMarkman hashtag at 3 p.m. EDT Thursday. If you can’t make it, visit the CancerCenter360 blog to view the complete recap of questions and answers each week.

If you have any questions about the process, just ask! Thank you for your support and participation!

Sincerely,
Your Cancer Fighters Team
