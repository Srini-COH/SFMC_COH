Jan loves to travel, golf, sail and spend quality time with family, friends and her fur baby Pebbles. Through her own personal health journey, she's learned to slow down, be present and focus on gratitude and resilience.

"My health journey was impacted with a meningioma brain tumor behind my right eye. How I learned about my tumor was when I contracted COVID-19 from my Mom in January of 2021, who lost her life trying to fight the virus. Despite the sadness of the loss of my Mom, it was a blessing she inadvertendly helped bring awareness to the tumor."
