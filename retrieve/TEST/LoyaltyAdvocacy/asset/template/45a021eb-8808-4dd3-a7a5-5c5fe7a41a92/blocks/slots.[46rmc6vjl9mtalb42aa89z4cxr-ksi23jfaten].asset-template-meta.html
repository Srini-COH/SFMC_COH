CTCA and City of Hope Have Created the Largest Bone Marrow and Blood Stem Cell Transplant Program in the United States

Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) is part of City of Hope®, one of the nation’s top 10 cancer hospitals and a pioneer in hematological malignancies and blood and marrow transplantation. Joining forces expands the City of Hope expertise to Chicago and Phoenix, creating the largest BMT program in the United States.
