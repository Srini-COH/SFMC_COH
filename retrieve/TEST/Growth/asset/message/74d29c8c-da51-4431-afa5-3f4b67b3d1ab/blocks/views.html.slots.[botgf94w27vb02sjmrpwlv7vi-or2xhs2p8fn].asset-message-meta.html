In the spirit of taking proactive health measures, early detection is a helpful defense against a potential cancer diagnosis, and we can help. At Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) in Zion, we continually strive to support the wellness of our community.

We help our neighbors here in Lake County and beyond by delivering comprehensive, personalized treatments to those diagnosed with cancer.

We also offer convenient access to routine screenings and can be your source for these important early detection assessments. A cancer screening is performed as a regular check for disease even in the absence of obvious signs or symptoms.
