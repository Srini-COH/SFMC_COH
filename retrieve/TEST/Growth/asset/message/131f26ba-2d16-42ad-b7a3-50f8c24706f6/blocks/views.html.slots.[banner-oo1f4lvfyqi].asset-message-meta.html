Does alcohol cause cancer?

Some people won't smoke cigarettes because they know tobacco use causes cancer. But they may not think twice about going out for drinks—oblivious to the cancer risk alcohol also poses. Despite stories of centenarians crediting their longevity to daily shots of whiskey, or studies attributing health benefits to red wine, leading cancer organizations now say there's no amount of alcohol that's safe to drink without increasing cancer risk. Fewer drinks are better than more drinks, but any drinking increases the risk for alcohol-related cancers.
