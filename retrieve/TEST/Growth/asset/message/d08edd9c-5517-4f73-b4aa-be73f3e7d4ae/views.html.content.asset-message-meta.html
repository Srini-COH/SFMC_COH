Genetic Change May Lead to More Aggressive Leukemia

 

Scientists with City of Hope and the Translational Genomics Research Institute, part of City of Hope, may have discovered one of the reasons why a type of leukemia sometimes develops into a more aggressive form, according to recent research published in Nature Communications. This breakthrough could pave the way for new treatments.
