Navigating health insurance options
as a cancer patient

As a cancer patient, you’re likely to face challenges you’ve never experienced, including the health care costs that come with diagnosis, treatment and lifestyle changes. It is important to understand your health insurance options and which benefits are available to you as a cancer patient, not only to your financial health, but also to your physical and mental health. During open enrollment season—the time when health insurance plans are accepting new and returning applicants—now is the time to understand your choices and make informed decisions to help avoid or mitigate unnecessary costs.
