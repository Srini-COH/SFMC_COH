While public vaccinations are not a norm for us, helping our neighbors here in Lake County during this global health crisis was too important; we knew we had to take action. We’d like to thank you for choosing Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA) in Zion, IL, for your COVID-19 vaccination.

In the spirit of taking proactive health measures, we want you to know that early detection is an important defense against a potential cancer diagnosis, and we can help.

According to a survey by the American Cancer Society Cancer Action Network (ACS CAN), more than two-thirds of Americans reported delaying or skipping their scheduled cancer screenings, such as mammograms and colonoscopies, during the COVID-19 crisis.

Here at CTCA® in Zion, we continually strive to support the wellness of our community—and we do that best by delivering comprehensive, personalized treatments to those who’ve been diagnosed with cancer. You may not know this, but we also offer convenient access to routine screenings, and we can be your source for these important early detection tools. A cancer screening is performed as a regular check for disease even in the absence of obvious signs or symptoms.

Learn more about available screenings at CTCA and eligibility requirements for getting screened with us, where you can expect quality, personalized care. Together, we can promote wellness in our community. Call us at 844-MY-FIGHT to get started.
