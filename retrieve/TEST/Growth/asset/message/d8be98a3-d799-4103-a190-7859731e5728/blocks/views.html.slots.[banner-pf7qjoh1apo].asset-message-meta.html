What is the Mother Standard® of care?
 
Treating cancer should be about more than fighting the disease. It’s about treating the whole patient, and recognizing that each person comes to the journey with his or her unique needs, challenges, preferences and goals. Whether it’s tailoring treatments to address both the disease and its side effects, or helping patients make educated decisions about their care, the Mother Standard offers a comprehensive, compassionate patient experience.
