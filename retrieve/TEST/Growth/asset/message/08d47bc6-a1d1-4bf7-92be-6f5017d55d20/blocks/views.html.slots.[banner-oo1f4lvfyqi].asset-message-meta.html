Managing cancer symptoms with medical marijuana:
What patients need to know

As more states approve medical marijuana laws, questions remain about the legitimate use of marijuana for medical conditions, including cancer. Marijuana may offer an option to manage cancer symptoms, but due to the lack of clinical evidence currently available, some health care providers advise patients to proceed with caution. Dr. Kevin Tulipana, Director of Hospital Medicine and Vice Chief of Staff at Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA), Tulsa, answers some questions that may shed some light on the use of medical marijuana.
