Thank you for being part of the Cancer Compass® community! We're writing to let you know that the platform where you initially signed up is evolving. The Cancer Compass experience will now be part of the Cancer Fighters® community.

This is a well-established, active online community where you'll have an opportunity to learn and engage with others affected by cancer. The message boards from our original site have transitioned into the Cancer Fighters community, so you can find all Compass discussions.

We'd love to have you join in and start a discussion about your cancer journey, ask or answer questions for support or join a special interest group.

What does this change mean?
This change means you'll need to create a username and password on the Cancer Fighters site to get started. The log-in information you used for Cancer Compass will not be transitioning over automatically. To continue to connect with the community, please create an account.
