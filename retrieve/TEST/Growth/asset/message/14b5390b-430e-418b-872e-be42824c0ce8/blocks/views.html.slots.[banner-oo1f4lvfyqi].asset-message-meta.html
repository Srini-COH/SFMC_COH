When it comes to cancer symptoms,
there's no such thing as TMI

Sharing doesn’t always mean caring, and some secrets, no matter how seemingly minor, may be best kept under wraps. When it comes to your health, however, sharing is caring (about yourself), and there's no such thing as too much information. Doctors need to know what you’re experiencing, if you have any symptoms—no matter how gross—and if they’re getting worse. That’s especially true with potential cancer symptoms. Identifying cancer symptoms early often results in more available and less invasive treatment options and stronger outcomes.
