What cancer patients need to know about the COVID-19 Delta variant

When the first COVID-19 vaccinations were administered in December 2020, experts hoped that would signal the beginning of the end of a pandemic that has killed more than 660,000 Americans. It seems that hope has been short-lived. The coronavirus Delta variant has taken hold, causing a surge in new cases, and requiring cancer patients and their caregivers to take extra steps to reduce their risk of exposure.
