Busting myths: Unraveling some of the mysteries and misconceptions about cancer and cancer treatment

For many patients and caregivers, cancer is a mystery. Where did it come from, and why is it so hard to treat? It was gone, but now it’s back—how can that be? For many, the search for answers may lead to facts that help solve some of the mysteries of cancer and help them make confident decisions on treatments and care. For others, research may lead to assumptions, misconceptions and myths that may have devastating consequences. To help resolve some of the confusion, the CTCA Blog developed an occasional series, called Busting myths. This compilation of myth-busting blogs is designed to help break through some of the misconceptions about cancer and cancer treatment.
