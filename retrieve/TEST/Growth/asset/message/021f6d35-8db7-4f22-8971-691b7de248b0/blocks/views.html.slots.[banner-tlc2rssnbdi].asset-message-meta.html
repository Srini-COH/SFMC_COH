Among the multiple diagnostics and treatments offered by our team is transoral robotic surgery.

Through this minimally invasive surgical approach, anatomical locations typically difficult to reach, including areas of the oropharynx, the hypopharynx and the larynx, can be accessed. Operation occurs directly through a patient’s natural orifices, reducing the necessity for a large incision in the neck, which may lead to a quicker recovery, shorter hospital stays, reduced post-surgical pain, reduced risk of infection and complications, and less blood loss and scarring. Currently, in my practice at CTCA Tulsa, we are one of only two hospitals in Oklahoma to offer transoral robotic surgery via the Flex Robotic System.
