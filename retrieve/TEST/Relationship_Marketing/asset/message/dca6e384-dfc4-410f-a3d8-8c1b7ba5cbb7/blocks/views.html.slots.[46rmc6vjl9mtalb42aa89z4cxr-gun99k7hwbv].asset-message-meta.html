The skin is the body's largest organ, and, like other organs, it can develop cancer. In honor of Melanoma and Skin Cancer Awareness Month, learn how to minimize risks against these cancer types.

One of the best proactive steps you can take to detect skin cancer early is to perform a self-exam. It's recommended to do once a month to familiarize yourself with any freckles, moles and other marks. Be sure to report changes to your doctor.
