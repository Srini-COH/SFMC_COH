As someone who has experienced the life-changing news of a cancer diagnosis, you know the importance of routine cancer screenings. Screenings help find cancer at an early stage, before symptoms appear. That makes screening one of the most powerful tools you have to reduce your risk of a second cancer or a recurrence.

If you’re like more than two-thirds of Americans*, you delayed or cancelled screening appointments―mammogram, colonoscopy, PAP/HPV test, skin check or other screening―during the COVID-19 pandemic. Now it’s critical to get back on track. Start today with the Cancer Screening Tracker.
