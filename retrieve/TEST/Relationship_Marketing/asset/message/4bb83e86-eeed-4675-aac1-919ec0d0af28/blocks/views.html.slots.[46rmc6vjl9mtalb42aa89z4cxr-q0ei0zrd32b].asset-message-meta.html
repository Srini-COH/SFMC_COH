The summer sunshine brings a golden opportunity to spotlight important steps you can take to minimize the risk that comes with sun exposure. Here are some simple tips for how you can protect yourself:

  • Avoid tanning salons and tanning beds.
  • Perform regular skin self-examinations—here's how.
  • Seek shade if you must be outside between the hours of 10am and 4pm, when the sun’s ultraviolet (UV) light is typically at its strongest.
  • Use a broad-spectrum sunscreen that protects against UVA and UVB rays with a sun protection factor (SPF) of 30 or higher. Re-apply sunscreen every two hours, or more if you are swimming or sweating.
