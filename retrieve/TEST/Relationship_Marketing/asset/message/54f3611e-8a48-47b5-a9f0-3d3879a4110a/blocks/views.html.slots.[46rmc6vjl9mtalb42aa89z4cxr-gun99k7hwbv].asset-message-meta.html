Cancer impacts the entire family. After battling cancer, it's important to find a way to heal and navigate a new normal together.

Use these resources as conversation starters to help those who are close to you learn more about what you may experience as a cancer survivor, and how they might be able to support you:

  • Can cancer come back? Common questions about recurrence
  • Scanxiety? What it is and how to cope with it
  • Taking control of your body image after cancer

Connecting with others can be empowering. When you need extra support or want to hear how others have endured similar situations, the Cancer Fighters® program is here for you.

We also offer survivorship support services as part of our patient-centered care model, connecting you with a team member for help with managing short-term, long-term and late side effects of cancer and its treatment.
