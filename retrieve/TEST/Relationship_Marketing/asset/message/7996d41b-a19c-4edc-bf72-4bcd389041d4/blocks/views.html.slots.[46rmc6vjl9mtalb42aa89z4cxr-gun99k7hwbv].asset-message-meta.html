If you've had a relative who has been diagnosed with cancer, that doesn't necessarily mean you'll develop the diseases, too, but it's a possibility if you have an inherited risk. That's why it's important to talk to your family to learn more about any health issues you may face in the future.

Keeping a detailed family history, especially going back a few generations, can help you and your doctor to establish a prevention plan that suits your needs. The Cancer Screening Tracker, developed by City of Hope experts, is an easy tool to capture pertinent information.
