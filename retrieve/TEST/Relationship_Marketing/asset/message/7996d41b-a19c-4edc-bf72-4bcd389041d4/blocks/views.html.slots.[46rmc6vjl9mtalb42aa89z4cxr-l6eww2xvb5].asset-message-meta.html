Questions for your family
Here are a few questions to help get the conversation going:

  • Has a relative in our family had cancer?
  • What type of cancer did they have?
  • How old was this family member when diagnosed?
Questions for your doctor
When talking to your doctor about your family history, or any current health concerns, you can also ask these questions:
  • Does the history of cancer in my family put me at higher risk for the disease?
  • Should I consider genetic testing?
