Early detection is key because cancers found in their earliest stages are typically easier to treat and are more likely to have positive outcomes. Protect yourself with preventative measures such as completing your recommended cancer screening tests and learning how to recognize concerning symptoms.

You can keep track of your screenings and family history in one convenient document using the Cancer Screening Tracker from Cancer Treatment Centers of America® (CTCA). Use this tool to help make a plan for appointments and help reduce your cancer risk.
