Cancer impacts the entire family. Finding a way to begin the healing process together helps you stay supported while also giving your loved ones tools to cope with your diagnosis.

Use these resources as conversation starters to help those who are close to you learn more about what you may experience in your cancer journey, and how they may be able to help:

  • Understanding cancer-related memory and cognition challenges
  • How cancer impacts patients' body image and other physical and emotional changes
  • Strategies for addressing cancer-related eating difficulties

At City of Hope, we offer behavioral health services as part of our patient-centered care model, connecting you with a therapist for help with managing stress and navigating relationship concerns, including support for communication and intimacy challenges.
