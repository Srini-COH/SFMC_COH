Do you have trouble remembering when to take your medication or where you left your glasses? Do you sometimes feel like you're in a fog?

If you answered yes to any of these questions, you may be coping with cancer-related cognitive impairment (CRCI), also known as "chemo brain," and you're not alone. Recent research shows that more than half of patients with cancer report having some sort of cognitive change during or after treatment.
